COVID-19 Salary Retained Fund — Deduction and Repayment Policy

Archived Decision: This Office Order was issued during the COVID-19 pandemic lockdown of 2020. The salary retained fund has since been repaid per OO/10/21. This document is maintained for historical reference only.

COVID-19 Salary Retained Fund

This Office Order was issued in response to the financial impact of the COVID-19 pandemic lockdown on school fee collection and operations. It outlines the decision to retain a portion of staff salaries in a managed fund and the conditions for repayment.

Background

The nation-wide lockdown from March 2020 resulted in school closures and a significant reduction in fee collection across all LMCS branches. To sustain school operations and protect long-term employment, the management took the decision to temporarily retain a portion of staff salaries in a COVID Fund, to be repaid once financial conditions normalised.

Salary Deduction Policy

  • A specified percentage of each staff member's monthly salary was retained in the COVID Fund for the duration of the lockdown period.
  • The deduction percentage varied by salary band — higher-earning staff contributed a higher percentage.
  • The retained amount was recorded for each staff member and maintained as a liability of the school.
  • Staff received the remaining salary after deduction regularly on the scheduled salary payment date.

Conditions for Repayment

  • The retained salary would be repaid to each staff member once the school's financial situation recovered to a sustainable level.
  • Repayment was conditional on the school resuming normal operations and fee collection returning to pre-COVID levels.
  • The management committed to beginning repayment within a reasonable period of financial recovery, without specifying a fixed date in this Order.

Non-Working Staff Rules

  • Staff who were unable to perform any work during the lockdown (e.g., support staff with no remote work assignment) were subject to a higher deduction rate as they contributed no productive output during the closure.
  • Staff must have completed a minimum of 12 months of physical (on-campus) work at LMCS before becoming eligible for any retained fund repayment.
  • Staff who resigned or were terminated before the repayment period would forfeit their share of the retained fund.

Communication to Staff

All staff received individual communication detailing the amount retained from their salary each month. The retained amounts were included in salary slips as a line item "COVID Fund Retention". Staff were assured of the management's commitment to repayment and encouraged to continue serving during the challenging period.
