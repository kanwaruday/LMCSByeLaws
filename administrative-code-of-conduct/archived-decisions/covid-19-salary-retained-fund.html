COVID-19 Salary Retained Fund

COVID-19 Salary Retained Fund

In these times of Covid-19 when the entire nation was locked down, LMS continued providing quality education through Online Classes and other tools. The lockdown also affected the Schools financially, as only a small percentage of overall fees was received. In light of this, the following was decided by the School Management:

Salary Deductions

  1. With effect from the salary of April 2020, the School deducts a part of each employee's salary and places it in the "Covid-19 Retained Fund", which is reflected on Salary Slips.
  2. Deductions may be both percentage-based and salary-capping-based.
  3. The part of salary in the Covid-19 Retained Fund is not lost — it will be paid to the employee once the School becomes financially comfortable and fee receivable normalises.

Teachers Working from Home

  1. The School pays the complete month's salary to teachers working from home, despite working hours being 4 hours instead of the usual 6.
  2. As teachers save travel time while working from home, the School has decided not to provide the 1 Casual Leave per month that teachers previously received.

Admin Staff

Admin Staff are requested to function closer to normal on the basis of frequent communication over WhatsApp Groups, given their crucial role in the School's finances.

Non-Working Staff (Helpers, Peon cum Drivers)

  1. Full salaries will be generated for drivers and other non-working staff despite them not working.
  2. However, they will be paid only up to the monthly designated salary cap — the remaining amount is secured in the Covid-19 Retained Fund.
  3. In exchange, whenever the School asks its employees to be present, all non-working staff must return to the School.
  4. The entire Covid-19 Retained Fund is payable to a non-working staff member under the following conditions:
    • The employee must report to the School whenever called.
    • The employee must physically work for a minimum of 12 months before becoming eligible for the Covid-19 Retained Fund.
    • If the employee leaves before the 12-month physical working period, no Covid-19 Retained Fund will be payable.
  5. Before completion of the 12-month physical working period, the School shall not accept the resignation of any non-working employee.
  6. Security payable by the School at the time of proper resignation shall be payable as per the resignation norms prior to acceptance.
