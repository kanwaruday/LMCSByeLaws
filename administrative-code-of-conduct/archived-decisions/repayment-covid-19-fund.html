Repayment of COVID-19 Salary Retained Fund

Archived Decision: This Office Order initiated the repayment of salaries retained during the COVID-19 lockdown of 2020. Repayment has been completed as per the schedules below. This document is maintained for historical reference only.

Repayment of COVID-19 Fund

This Office Order initiates the formal repayment of the COVID-19 Salary Retained Fund to all eligible staff members, following the financial recovery of the school. The repayment is structured in priority order to ensure the most financially vulnerable staff are repaid first.

Repayment Priority Structure

PriorityStaff CategoryRationale
Priority 1Support and non-teaching staff at the lowest salary bandGreatest financial hardship during the lockdown period; dependent on salary for day-to-day expenses
Priority 2Teaching staff at junior and mid-level salary bandsModerate financial impact; significant retained amounts due to longer lockdown deduction periods
Priority 3Senior teaching staff and department headsHigher capacity to absorb the deferral; retained amounts to be repaid after Priority 1 and 2 are complete
Priority 4Administrative managers and PrincipalsHighest salary band; repaid last once all other categories have received full repayment

Instalment Plans by Staff Category

Repayment is structured in instalments added to the monthly salary. The instalment period depends on the total retained amount and the staff category:

Staff CategoryInstalment PeriodMethod
Priority 1 (Support / Non-teaching)12 months (1 year)Monthly addition to salary; equal instalments
Priority 2 (Teaching — Junior/Mid)24 months (2 years)Monthly addition to salary; equal instalments
Priority 3 (Senior Teaching / Dept. Heads)24–36 months (2–3 years)Monthly addition to salary; instalments as per individual agreement
Priority 4 (Admin Managers / Principals)36 months (3 years)Monthly addition to salary; equal instalments commencing after Priority 1–3 repayments begin

Repayment Conditions

  • Repayment commences from April 2021 for Priority 1 staff and is staggered for subsequent priority levels.
  • Staff who resign during the repayment period will receive any remaining unpaid balance as a lump sum in their Full & Final settlement, subject to available school funds.
  • Staff who joined after the COVID lockdown period and therefore have no retained fund balance are not included in this scheme.
  • The exact retained amount for each staff member was communicated individually by the Finance team along with the repayment schedule.
  • Any discrepancy in the retained amount must be raised with the Finance team within 30 days of receiving the individual communication.

Acknowledgement

The management acknowledges the cooperation and patience of all staff during the COVID-19 lockdown period. This repayment scheme fulfils the commitment made in OO/06/20 to return the retained amounts once the school's financial position allowed.
