Repayment of COVID-19 Retained Fund

Repayment of COVID-19 Retained Fund

In the new Academic & Financial Year, the School Management expects parents to pay their Annual Fees. The Annual Fees received is to be utilised in the following priority order:

Priority 1 — Repayment of the Covid-19 Retained Fund for Employees

CategoryRepayment Terms
Teachers & Admin Staff joined before 31 December 2019 2 months' CTI converted from Covid-19 Fund to Security; remainder released in 12 monthly instalments
Teachers & Admin Staff with RRF (joined after 1 January 2020) Entire Covid-19 Fund released in 24 monthly instalments
Helping Staff before RRF 2 months' CTI converted to Security; remainder released in 12 monthly instalments
Helping Staff after RRF Entire Covid-19 Fund released in 24 monthly instalments
Non-working Helping Staff / Driver cum Peons — LMS 1, 2 & 4 3 months' CTI converted to Security; remainder released in 36 monthly instalments
Non-working Helping Staff / Driver cum Peons — LMS 3 3 months' CTI converted to Security; remainder released in 24 monthly instalments

Priority 2 — Resigned Staff

Payment of the Security / RRF of staff who have already left following all resignation norms.

Priority 3 — School Vendors

Payment to all School vendors who have provided materials to the School on credit.

Priority 4 — Unsecured Loan

Repayment of the unsecured loan given by the Founder Chairman of Hidimba Educational Society.
