Fee Structure 2023

LMS 4 Sibling Pricing

LMS 4 introduced a sibling pricing policy for academic year 2023–24. Families with more than one child enrolled at LMS 4 receive a discount on the monthly fee for the second and subsequent children:

  • First child: Full fee as per LMS 4 schedule
  • Second child: Reduced fee as per sibling discount notification
  • Third child and beyond: Further concession as per Principal's discretion with MD approval

Sibling discount is applicable only when all children are simultaneously enrolled and attending LMS 4.

Montessori Fee

Montessori level (M1–M3) fees are maintained separately and vary by branch. Contact the respective branch office for Montessori fee details for 2023.

Annual & Other Charges

  • Annual Development Fee: As notified per branch
  • DA/OD Assessment Fees: As per branch-level schedules for 2023–24
  • New Admission charges: As per branch office notification
