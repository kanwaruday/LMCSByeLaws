Fee Structure 2025 — LMS 6 (Joginder Nagar)

This Office Order notifies the fee structure for the newly established GI LMS branch at Joginder Nagar (LMS 6). An early-bird admission discount is available for the inaugural session.

Early-Bird Admission Discount

An early-bird discount of 75% on the first month's tuition fee is offered to students who confirm admission before the inaugural session cutoff date. This discount applies to:

  • All new admissions to LMS 6 for the inaugural academic session
  • The first month's fee only; subsequent months are charged at the full rate
  • Students must pay the registration fee in full to be eligible for the early-bird discount

About LMS 6 — GI LMS Joginder Nagar

LMS 6 is the sixth school in the La Montessori Chain, established at Joginder Nagar in 2025. The branch follows the LMCS curriculum and bye laws applicable to all branches. GI (Government Integrated) status means the branch operates in partnership with local government education authorities.

For exact amounts and the complete fee schedule for LMS 6, contact the LMS 6 branch office or Head Office Finance with reference to OO/03/25.
