Fee Structure for Academic Year 2022–23

Fee Structure 2022

This Office Order notifies the revised fee structure applicable to all LMCS branches for the academic year 2022–23.

Monthly Tuition Fee

WingMonthly FeeClasses
Primary Wing₹ 2,450Class I – Class V
Elementary Wing₹ 2,550Class VI – Class VIII
Senior Wing₹ 2,650Class IX – Class X

LMS 4 Discount

Students enrolled at LMS 4 are eligible for a 66% discount on the standard monthly tuition fee applicable to their wing. This discount reflects the LMS 4 branch fee structure which is maintained at a lower rate to serve the local community.

WingStandard FeeLMS 4 Fee (after 66% discount)

Montessori Fee

Montessori level (M1–M3) fees are maintained separately and vary by branch. Contact the respective branch office for Montessori fee details for 2022.

Annual & Other Charges

  • Annual Development Fee: As per the 2021 schedule (no change notified in OO/01/22)
  • DA/OD Assessment Fees: As per branch-level schedules for 2022–23
  • New Admission charges: As per branch office notification
