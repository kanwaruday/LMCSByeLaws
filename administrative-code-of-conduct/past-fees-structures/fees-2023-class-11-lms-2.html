Class 11 Opening and Fee Structure — LMS Kelheli (LMS 2)

Class 11 Fee Structure 2023 – LMS 2 (Kelheli)

This Office Order notifies the opening of Class 11 at La Montessori School Kelheli (LMS 2) and sets the fee structure for the three academic streams offered.

Streams Offered

  • Humanities — English, History, Political Science, Geography / Economics, Optional Subject
  • Commerce — English, Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, Optional Subject
  • Science — English, Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics / Biology, Optional Subject

Monthly Fee Structure

StreamMonthly FeeNotes
HumanitiesAs per OO/04/23 scheduleIncludes tuition and activity fee
CommerceAs per OO/04/23 scheduleIncludes tuition and activity fee
ScienceAs per OO/04/23 schedule (higher due to lab fees)Includes tuition, lab and activity fee
Exact monthly fee amounts for each stream are maintained in the LMS 2 branch records. Contact LMS 2 branch office or Head Office Finance for precise figures.

Scholarship Tiers

Scholarship concessions are available for Class 11 admissions at LMS 2 based on Class 10 board examination performance:

Class 10 ResultScholarshipEligibility Condition
90% and aboveScholarship Tier 1 (highest concession)Valid for students from any recognised school; subject to seat availability
80% – 89%Scholarship Tier 2 (medium concession)Valid for external students; LMS students get preferential processing
70% – 79%Scholarship Tier 3 (partial concession)Merit-based; limited seats
Below 70%No scholarship; full fee applicable—

Admission Process

  • Admission to Class 11 requires submission of Class 10 mark sheet and passing certificate.
  • Scholarship applications must be submitted with the admission form.
  • Scholarship approval is at the discretion of the Principal in consultation with MD Academics.
  • Seat allotment is on a first-come, first-served basis subject to stream availability.
