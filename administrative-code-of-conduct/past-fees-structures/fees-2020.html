Fee Structure 2020

The 2020 fee structure was maintained separately for each sub-school branch (LMS 1, LMS 2, LMS 3 and LMS 4). Detailed fee tables for each branch are stored in the respective branch administrative records.

Archived Record: The 2020 fee structure documents are maintained at the branch level and in the Head Office finance archive. The Notion source for this year contains sub-page links for each LMS branch rather than consolidated fee tables. Contact the Head Office Finance team or the respective branch Principal for branch-specific 2020 fee details.

Branches Covered

  • LMS 1 — La Montessori School, Branch 1
  • LMS 2 — La Montessori School, Kelheli
  • LMS 3 — La Montessori School, Branch 3
  • LMS 4 — La Montessori School, Branch 4

Context

Academic Year 2020 was significantly impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic. Fee collection was affected by school closures and the management issued specific guidance on fee payment during the lockdown period. Please refer to the COVID-19 Salary Retained Fund archived decision for relevant context.
