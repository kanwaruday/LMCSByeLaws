Fee Structure for Academic Year 2025–26 — LMS 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5

Fee Structure 2025 — LMS 1–5

This Office Order notifies the revised fee structure for academic year 2025–26 covering LMS branches 1 through 5. This is the first fee order to include LMS 5 and also introduces Class 11–12 fee structures.

LMS 5 — Monthly Tuition Fee

LMS 5 is included in the fee schedule for the first time from academic year 2025–26. Fee rates for LMS 5 are set at the branch level and are available from the LMS 5 Principal or Head Office Finance.

EA Fee Structure 2025–26

EA (External Assessment) fees continue as per the framework introduced in OO/01/24. Five EA cycles are scheduled for academic year 2025–26. Refer to EA Implementation 2025 for full details.

Annual Charges
