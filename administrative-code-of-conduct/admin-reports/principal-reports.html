Principal Reporting Format and Google Calendar Requirements

Principal Reports

This Office Order mandates the use of Google Calendar for daily scheduling and introduces a standardised reporting format for all school Principals across LMCS branches.

Google Calendar — Mandatory Use

All Principals are required to maintain an active Google Calendar for their branch. The following rules apply:

  • Google Calendar use is mandatory for all Principals with immediate effect from the date of this Order.
  • The next day's schedule must be uploaded to the branch Google Calendar by 5:00 PM every working day.
  • The schedule must include: morning assembly plan, class observations, staff meetings, parent meetings, administrative tasks and any special events.
  • The calendar must be shared with MD Academics and the Head Office Administrator.
  • Failure to upload the schedule in advance will be noted in the Principal's performance record.

Reporting Format

The standardised Principal's daily report must be submitted to MD Academics by end of working day. The report follows a three-section format:

Section 1 — Ongoing Tasks

A bullet-point list of tasks that are currently in progress but not yet completed. Each item must include:

  • Task description
  • Start date
  • Expected completion date
  • Current status / percentage completion
  • Any blockers or support required

Section 2 — Completed Tasks

A bullet-point list of tasks completed during the current day or since the last report. Each item must include:

  • Task description
  • Completion date
  • Brief outcome or result

Section 3 — Important Meetings

A summary of all significant meetings held during the day. For each meeting, the report must include:

  • Meeting type (staff meeting / parent meeting / administrative review / external visitor)
  • Participants
  • Key discussion points
  • Decisions made or action items arising
  • Follow-up required (if any) and responsible person

Submission Method

  • Reports are submitted via email from the Principal's official LMCS email ID to MD Academics.
  • Subject line format: [Branch Name] Principal Report – [DD/MM/YYYY]
  • Reports may also be entered into the designated shared Google Sheet where applicable.

Compliance

  • Non-submission of the daily report without prior intimation to MD Academics is treated as a dereliction of administrative duty.
  • Consistent non-compliance will be escalated to the Management Director and may affect the Principal's performance evaluation.
