Principal Reporting Format and Google Calendar Requirements

Principal Reports

Google Calendar — Mandatory Use

  1. Principals are expected to make extensive use of Google Calendar.
  2. School employees who are Principals or above must upload the next day's calendar schedule one day in advance.

New Reporting Format for Principals

The following three-section format is mandatory for all Principal reports:

  1. Ongoing Tasks
  2. Tasks Completed
  3. Important Meetings & Messages
    • Who did you meet, main agenda
