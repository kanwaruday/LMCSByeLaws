Fee Defaulter Identification and Follow-Up Process

Fee Defaulter Handling

The following steps must be taken every month to ensure smooth handling of Fee Defaulters:

Role of Fee Clerks

  1. Generate a Fee Defaulter List on the 14th of every month.
  2. Forward the list to:
    • The Admin Manager – tilak.sharma@lms.org.in
    • Principal of the respective School
  3. Send email to all fee defaulters on the 14th of every month.
  4. Make phone calls to fee-defaulting parents above certain limits, with effect from the 15th of each month.
  5. Contact parents as per the thresholds below and record all communication in the Parent Communication Register before the 25th of every month:
    BranchThreshold
    LMS 1Rs. 8,000
    LMS 2Rs. 8,000
    LMS 3Rs. 4,000
    LMS 4Rs. 7,000

Role of Admin Manager

  1. Compile the Fee Defaulter List and forward to Accounts Coordinator & MD Operations latest by the 15th of each month.
  2. Analyse the fee-defaulting numbers.
  3. Guide the Fee Clerks and ensure accountability.
  4. Track fee payments with the Fee Clerks, ensuring fee collection.
  5. Set up a fee meeting on the 26th of each month with the Accounts Coordinator.

Role of Principal

  1. For fee defaulters above Rs. 15,000, the Principal must inform the student about outstanding fees as follows:
    • The student must be spoken to individually and politely.
    • The student should be requested to remind their parents about the fees.
  2. These conversations must be undertaken between the 15th & 25th of each month.
  3. The Principal must present the Fee Defaulters list at their monthly Governing Body PTA meetings. The names of defaulters must be part of the proceedings.
  4. The Principal must submit a report of Fee Defaulters by the 26th of each month.

Role of Account Coordinator

  1. Ensure that all timelines mentioned above are followed.
  2. On the 26th of each month, meet with the Admin Manager, evaluate progress on fee collection, and confirm entries with the School Bank Accounts and Software.
