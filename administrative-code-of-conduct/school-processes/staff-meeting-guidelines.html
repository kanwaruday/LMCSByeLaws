Staff Meeting Timings and Scheduling Guidelines

Staff Meeting Guidelines

This Office Order prescribes the permitted timings for various types of staff meetings and interactions at LMCS schools to ensure that school operations, academic delivery and staff welfare are not disrupted.

Permitted Meeting Timings

Morning Assembly

The morning assembly is used for brief announcements and notice-sharing with students and staff. Administrative messages relevant to all staff may be communicated during morning assembly. Detailed discussions or decisions must not take place during assembly time.

Lunch Break

Quick informal catch-ups and clarifications between the Principal and individual staff members may take place during the lunch break. However, formal staff meetings or review sessions must not be scheduled during the lunch break, as staff are entitled to a rest period.

Free Periods

Subject-specific discussions, one-on-one reviews and small group consultations may be scheduled during free periods. Free periods must not be used for whole-school staff meetings. Staff are expected to use free periods productively for lesson planning, corrections and preparation.

Activity Teacher Consultation Before Events

Before any school event, cultural programme, sports day or competition is finalised, the Principal must consult with the Activity Teacher or the staff member responsible for organising the event. This consultation must happen:

  • At least 2 weeks before major events (Annual Day, Sports Day, Cultural Competition)
  • At least 1 week before smaller events (classroom presentations, inter-class competitions)

No event may be announced to students or parents without this prior consultation and the Principal's formal approval of the event plan.

Administrative Tasks & Parent/MD Meetings: 3–5 PM Window

The period from 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM is the designated slot for:

  • Administrative and operational tasks requiring focused time (report preparation, register checking, timetable updates)
  • Parent meetings — all parent-teacher meetings, parent grievance discussions and fee-related parent interactions
  • MD / Management Director meetings — any meeting called by MD with the Principal or administrative staff

Teaching staff should not be pulled out of teaching periods for administrative tasks during school hours. All such work is reserved for the 3–5 PM window.

Combined Staff Meetings: 4–5 PM

Full staff meetings or combined meetings involving all teaching and administrative staff must be scheduled between 4:00 PM and 5:00 PM. Rules for combined meetings:

  • Notice must be given at least 24 hours in advance for regular meetings; emergency meetings may be called with 2 hours' notice via official WhatsApp group.
  • An agenda must be shared before the meeting. Items not on the agenda should not be added without the Principal's consent at the start of the meeting.
  • Meeting minutes must be recorded and shared with all attendees within 24 hours of the meeting.
  • The meeting must conclude by 5:00 PM. Meetings that run over time require prior consent from MD Academics.
  • Attendance at combined meetings is mandatory for all staff unless prior leave has been approved.

Online/Virtual Meetings

Meetings with MD Academics or Head Office may be conducted via video call when in-person meeting is not feasible. Virtual meetings follow the same timing and agenda rules as in-person meetings. The Principal is responsible for ensuring all relevant staff have the meeting link and a functional device.
