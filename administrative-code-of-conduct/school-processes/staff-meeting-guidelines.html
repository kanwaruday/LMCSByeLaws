Principal Staff Meeting Guidelines

Staff Meeting Guidelines

Guidelines for Staff Meetings

  1. Meetings of a small group of teachers should preferably be held within School timings.
    Possible timings for short & small meetings:
    • Morning Assembly Time — 8:55 am to 9:20 am
    • Lunch Break — 12:20 pm to 12:40 pm
    • Free period of the teacher
  2. The Principal must meet the activity teachers before any event planned at the School to ensure smooth event management.
  3. The time between 3 pm & 5 pm may be used to complete School-related tasks, meet parents, and discuss matters with MDs.
  4. In case a Combined Teachers' Meeting is required, it may be held between 4 pm & 5 pm.
