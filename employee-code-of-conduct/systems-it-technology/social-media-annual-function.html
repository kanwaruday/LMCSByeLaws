Social Media Plan — Annual Function

Guidelines for uploading Annual Function pictures of respective schools to Facebook.

Responsibility Matrix

Task Responsible
Collection of photos from the photographerSocial Media Head
Sorting of picturesIT Teachers (can take help of Class Teachers)
School watermarksIT Teachers
Uploading on FacebookIT Teachers
Sharing Facebook link with parentsClass Teacher / Performance Incharge

Step 1: Sorting of Pictures

  • Sort into different folders according to the Performance List followed during the function
  • Every performance should have 40–50 pictures (for average 35–40 students per performance)
  • Performances with fewer than 25 students: minimum 25 pictures

Step 2: School Watermarks

  • Pictures should only be posted after the Social Media Head has approved the designated logo and advertisement
  • Use Lightroom / Canva / Photoshop for bulk adding of school logo and advertisement

Step 3: Uploading Guidelines

  • Upload in accordance with the Performance List sequence from the function
  • Posting begins one week prior to Dussehra
  • Post 2 performances daily (14 total) to complete Annual Function posts before Dussehra, allowing boosted posts during the break
  • Post 1: 9 AM – 12 PM  |  Post 2: 5 PM – 8 PM (ensure appropriate gap between the two)
  • On Dussehra: share posts related to respective school events, not Annual Function
  • If any performances remain, upload them during the Dussehra break
  • Once all performances are posted: upload Diya Lighting Ceremony, Guest candids, Group pictures, Award Ceremony, and speeches by the Principal and Management

After posting, Class Teachers must share the Facebook link via email with parents to boost engagement.
