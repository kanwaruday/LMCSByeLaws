Teachers Job Roles at Houses

Major events (involving Guests / Parents / External students) are organised by all four houses. All other school-level events are managed by one house at a time on a rotational basis.

Activity Teacher

  • Plan and allocate events to houses in consultation with the Principal
  • Send monthly email to students with the upcoming month's competitions and events
  • Prepare judgement sheets with the help of the IT Teacher
  • Maintain records of activities, winners, and participations in Activity Registers
  • Ensure winners are felicitated with certificates
  • Ensure all events and felicitations are uploaded to Social Media

PTI

  • Prepare the venue for events along with all logistical requirements
  • Undertake training for sports events
  • Help houses in formation of teams

IT Teacher

  • Provide all IT-related support for houses and school staff
  • Prepare the venue for events with respect to technical support
  • Ensure all events are uploaded to Social Media

House Moderator (Senior)

  • Serve as primary point of contact for students within the house (senior and junior)
  • Oversee organisation and execution of house-related activities and events
  • Communicate effectively with all house members
  • Promote student involvement in extra-curricular activities
  • Ensure discipline among house members; take productive calls on urgent matters
  • Take regular updates from captains and vice-captains, ensuring transparency

House Moderator (Junior)

  • Event planning and management (in consultation with Senior House Moderator)
  • Provide regular updates and reports to Senior House Moderator (keeping captains and vice-captains in the loop)
  • Serve as point of contact in the absence of the Senior House Moderator

House Secretary

  • Maintain records of meetings, activities, and decisions made by the house
  • Serve as link between house members and moderators (physical and through WhatsApp groups)
  • Maintain an updated list of house members with contact numbers (including teachers)
  • Take care of finances (during fete, school fair, etc.)
  • Delegate responsibilities to teachers for various events (keeping moderators in the loop)

Teacher House Members

  • Actively support house-related activities and events (sports, cultural, academic, and others)
  • Assist House Moderators in planning and execution of events
  • Ensure student participation and motivation within the house
  • Help maintain discipline and house spirit
  • Take on specific responsibilities assigned by House Moderators and House Secretary
