Procedure for Selection of School Appointees

The following selection rules apply after the Investiture Ceremony competitions are completed. See Investiture Ceremony 2026 for full competition details.

Selection Order

PositionSelection Criteria
Student Representative Top scorer across all competitions
House Captains (Class 10 & Class 6) Top 4 scorers of Classes 10 & 6 (after Student Representative selection)
House Vice Captains (Class 9 & Class 5) Top 4 scorers of Classes 9 & 5
House Editorial Prefect After House Captain selection — remaining top 4 declamation competition winners from Classes 9 or 10
House Sports Prefect After House Captain selection — remaining top 4 physical test winners from Classes 9 or 10
