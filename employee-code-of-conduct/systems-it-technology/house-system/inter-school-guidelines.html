Guidelines for Inter-School Competitions

General Rules (All Schools)

  1. Principal and concerned teachers (PTI / Subject Teacher) travel with students. A maximum of one additional teacher per participating wing is permitted if the Principal sees the need.
  2. Teachers must accompany and stay with their own students throughout — no separate teacher space is to be created.
  3. Participating teams must be accompanied by School Appointees.
  4. No transport fee shall be charged for students representing their school.
  5. Students carry their own lunch boxes and water bottles.
  6. Students must cheer for their school to the best of their abilities.

Host School Additional Requirements

  1. Besides the regular teacher allocation, Host School must include the Activity Teacher, IT Teacher, and PTI
  2. Host School must welcome Guest Schools to the best of their abilities
  3. Host School must clarify competition rules 1 month before the competition
  4. Fixtures and timelines must be shared by the Host School 2 days in advance

Photography & Documentation

  • Both Host and Guest Schools are expected to take care of photography
  • Punctuality must be followed; defaulting teams shall be penalised
  • Students must be encouraged to make placards to support their school
  • Each school should nominate commentators in sync with the Editorial Prefects

School Influencers (Social Media)

  • Students in the contingent are encouraged to post pictures/videos from their personal social media accounts
  • Each post/story/video must tag the school's Instagram page: @lamontessorischool
  • Guidelines: No vulgarity (verbal or physical); aesthetically pleasing pictures; images must convey fun, enjoyment, and happiness; students must tag their friends

Pre-Competition Preparation

  • Schools must conduct Inter-House Competitions two weeks before an Inter-School Competition to screen the most effective school team (can use a Special Day with 2 hours)
  • The school team must be announced and showcased to the entire school at least 1 day before the competition during the Morning Assembly
  • The winning team must be praised during the Morning Assembly after the competition
