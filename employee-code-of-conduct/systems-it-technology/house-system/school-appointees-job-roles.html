School Appointees Job Roles

Student Representative (Schools up to Class 12)

  • Reports to the Activity Teacher
  • Head of the Student Organising Committee for every school event
  • Helps teachers maintain discipline during school events

Band Leader — LMS 2 only (Class 9/10)

  • Must be an NCC Cadet
  • Heads the School Scottish Band, its equipment, and team
  • Facilitates band training

House Captain (Class 10 & Class 6)

  • Leader of the house
  • Responsible for coordination between students and teachers for all events

House Vice Captain (Class 9 & Class 5)

  • Supports the House Captain in all house activities

House Sports Prefect (Class 9/10)

  • Responsible for discipline of the school
  • Leads sports activities and promotes participation
  • Assists the PTI with organising sports equipment and facilities

House Editorial Prefect (Class 9/10)

  • Works in close coordination with the House Secretary Teacher
  • Responsible for anchoring and compering for Inter-House events
  • Mentors fellow housemates for oratory skills
  • Involved in decoration, artwork, and invites for house-organised events
  • Oversees and manages all written content produced by the house, ensuring alignment with the school's brand voice and objectives
