House System & School Competitions

This office order establishes a unified framework for all co-curricular and sports activities across La Montessori Schools. It covers house allocation, job roles, competition marking schemes, and selection procedures for student leaders.

🏠

House Allocation

4 houses (Emerald, Ruby, Sapphire, Topaz); teacher and student allocation rules; ERST rotation system
👔

Teachers Job Roles

Activity Teacher, PTI, IT Teacher, House Moderators (Senior/Junior), House Secretary, and Teacher House Members
🎖️

School Appointees Job Roles

Student Representatives, Band Leader, House Captains, Vice Captains, Sports Prefect, Editorial Prefect responsibilities
📊

Marking Scheme — All Competitions

Points scale: Class Level to International Level; CSM portal integration for tracking
📋

Selection Procedure

How House Captains, Vice Captains, Editorial Prefects and Sports Prefects are selected after the Investiture competitions
🏆

Inter-School Guidelines

Conduct rules for inter-school competitions: travel, host school duties, social media, inter-house screening
🔢

Inter-School Selection

4-step process: Class Level → Wing Level → Inter-House → School Team selection
