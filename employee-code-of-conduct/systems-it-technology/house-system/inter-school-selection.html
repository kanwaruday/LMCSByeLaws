Selection Procedure for Inter-School Competitions

Step 1: Class Level Competition

  • Class Teachers remind students about each upcoming event at least 1 month prior
  • Motivate students to participate
  • Class Teacher selects the top 3 students per class for the Wing Level Competition

Step 2: Wing Level Competition

  • Top 3 students from each class participate
  • The top 3 students from each wing are declared the wing winners
  • These students are given priority for Inter-House and Inter-School competitions

Step 3: Inter-House Competition

  • House Captain and Vice Captain, in consultation with House Moderators, form teams for Inter-House Competitions
  • Best players from all houses are identified and given priority for the Inter-School Competition

Step 4: Selection of Inter-School Team

  • Best students from the Inter-House Competitions are selected for the School Teams
These norms are designed to ensure a smooth and efficient conduct of events and competitions while maintaining high standards and accountability among teachers and coordinators.
