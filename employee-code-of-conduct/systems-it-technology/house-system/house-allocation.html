Teacher House Allocation

  • All school teachers are part of the school houses, except: Activity Teacher, IT Teacher, and PTI
  • Teachers must be equally allocated across the four houses
  • Teachers are free to choose their house. Each house must include at least 1 teacher from Foundational Stages, Primary Wing, and Senior Wing
  • House composition can be changed each year

Student House Allocation

  • Students are allocated houses when they enter Class 3, based on Academic Performance from Class 2
  • Students are reshuffled when they enter Class 7, based on Overall Performance from Class 6
  • Class 2 and Class 6 student scores are sorted in ascending order
  • Houses are allocated in the order ERST on a leap year; subsequent years follow RSTE, STER, TERS rotation
