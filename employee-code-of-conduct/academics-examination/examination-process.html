Examination Process

This manual codifies the complete examination workflow for La Montessori Schools. It prescribes mandatory question-paper formatting, submission routes and timelines; defines a multi-layer review, printing, storage and distribution chain; and sets clear responsibilities for teachers, IT staff, coordinators, administrators and principals. Penalties are detailed for late or erroneous work, from drafting to super-checking and mark entry.
