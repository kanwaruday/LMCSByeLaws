Report Card Printing

Report cards are generated from the CSM portal and distributed to branches through a defined chain of responsibility.

Printing & Distribution Chain

Step Responsible Action
1 IT Teacher Generates PDF report cards from the CSM portal for all students, branch-wise. Verifies data accuracy against submitted marks and remarks.
2 System Coordinator Reviews the generated PDFs for completeness and formatting. Approves batch for printing and coordinates the physical printing process.
3 Admin Manager Receives printed report cards branch-wise. Coordinates distribution to each branch Principal for delivery to parents.
4 Branch Principal Distributes report cards to class teachers, who hand them to parents/guardians on the designated report card day.

Deadline

Report cards must be printed and ready for distribution by the last working day of the term.

Important Rules

  • Report cards must not be handed to students directly — they must always go to a parent or authorised guardian.
  • If a parent has an outstanding fee dues, the report card may be withheld at the Principal's discretion pending clearance.
  • Digital copies (PDF) may be shared via the school communication system only with written permission from the MD Operations.
  • Any error found after printing must be corrected via a Rectification Sheet and re-printed at the earliest — the incorrect version must be retrieved and destroyed.
