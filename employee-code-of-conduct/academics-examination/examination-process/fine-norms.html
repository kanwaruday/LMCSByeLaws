Fine Norms

Fines are levied for violations at various stages of the examination process. All fines are deducted directly from salary.

Question Paper Submission Fines

Violation Fine
Late submission of QP (per day, per paper) Rs. 50 / day / paper
QP not submitted by the examination date Rs. 1,000 + Rs. 1,000 (i.e., Rs. 2,000 total)

Formatting Fines

Violation Fine
Incorrect font or font size Rs. 50 per paper
Incorrect margin settings Rs. 50 per paper
Other formatting mistakes in question paper Rs. 5 per mistake

Answer Sheet & Correction Fines

Violation Fine
Late return of answer sheets (per day) Rs. 50 / day
Marking mistake found during super-checking Rs. 1 per mistake
Fines accumulate. A teacher who submits a QP 5 days late with 3 formatting errors would incur: Rs. 250 (late) + Rs. 15 (formatting) = Rs. 265. All fines are tracked by the System Coordinator and reported to the Admin Manager for salary processing.
