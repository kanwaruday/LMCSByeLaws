Examination Day Process

Timings, protocols, and responsibilities for invigilators, teachers, and the System Coordinator on each day of the examination.

Key Timings

Time Activity
8:45 AM Question papers distributed to invigilators; seating verified
9:00 AM Examination begins; question papers handed to students
9:30 AM No student allowed entry after this time; late arrivals forfeit the examination

Post-Examination Protocol

Step Process Responsible
1 Correction: Answer sheets checked by the subject teacher of the branch where the student studies Subject Teacher
2 Counter-School Exchange: Answer sheets are exchanged between counter schools for independent verification System Coordinator
3 Super-Checking: A senior teacher reviews marks using a black pen only. Any change must be initialled HOD / Senior Teacher
4 Rectification Sheet: A Rectification Sheet is filled for every mark change identified during super-checking Super-Checking Teacher
5 Virtual Meeting: Held if content errors are found in the question paper (error in QP affects all students uniformly) System Coordinator + Principal
6 Final CSM Update: Marks entered into the CSM portal only after the Principal signs off on the Rectification Sheet IT Teacher
Super-checking must be done using a black pen exclusively. Any corrections made in a different colour or without initials will not be accepted and must be redone.
