Submission & Correction Process

Question papers must be submitted well in advance and go through a structured multi-stage review chain before they are approved for printing.

Submission Deadline

Question papers must be submitted to the HOD at least 40 days prior to the first day of the examination. Late submissions attract fines as per the Fine Norms.

Review Chain

Stage Responsible Person Action
1 Subject Teacher Prepares question paper and submits to HOD
2 HOD / Senior Subject Teacher Reviews for content accuracy and blueprint compliance
3 Subject Teacher Incorporates HOD feedback and resubmits
4 IT Teacher Checks formatting (fonts, margins, layout)
5 System Coordinator Final review, approval, and printing coordination

Daily Timeline During Examination

The following timeline governs each examination day from correction to final mark upload:

Activity Timeline
Answer sheets distributed to respective teachers for checking Same day after exam
Teachers complete checking (see QP Checking Time) Within 1–2 days
Super-checking by HOD / senior teacher using black pen After initial checking
Rectification Sheet filled for any mark changes During super-checking
Virtual meeting (if content errors found) As required
Final marks updated in CSM portal after Principal approval After rectification sign-off
No mark may be updated in the CSM portal without the Principal's written approval on the Rectification Sheet. Unauthorised mark changes are treated as a serious misconduct.
