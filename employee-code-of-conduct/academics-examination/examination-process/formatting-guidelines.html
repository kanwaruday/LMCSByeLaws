Formatting Guidelines

All question papers must strictly follow the formatting specifications below. Non-compliance attracts fines as listed.

Font Specifications by Subject

Subject Font Size
English Bookman Old Style 11 pt
Hindi KrutiDev10 14 pt
Sanskrit KrutiDev10 14 pt
Mathematics Times New Roman 12 pt
Computer Bookman Old Style 11 pt
All other subjects Bookman Old Style 11 pt

Margins

All four margins (Top, Bottom, Left, Right) must be set to 0.63 inches for every question paper.

Fine Structure for Formatting Errors

Violation Fine
Incorrect font or font size Rs. 50 per paper
Incorrect margin settings Rs. 50 per paper
Other formatting mistakes in QP Rs. 5 per mistake
Formatting errors discovered after printing will still attract the full fine. Teachers are responsible for proofreading their question papers before submission to the System Coordinator.
