Printing Schedule

The System Coordinator is responsible for coordinating the printing, distribution, and secure storage of all question papers across LMCS branches.

Responsibilities

  • The System Coordinator manages the complete printing schedule for each examination cycle.
  • The System Coordinator must share the final printing schedule with the Admin Manager at least one week prior to the examination start date.
  • Printed question papers must be stored in a secure room with restricted access until the day of the examination.
  • Access to printed question papers before the examination is strictly limited to the System Coordinator and, where necessary, the Admin Manager.
  • Any breach of question paper confidentiality will be treated as a serious misconduct matter and referred to the MD Operations.

Distribution Protocol

  • Question papers are distributed to individual branches on the morning of the examination or as per the pre-approved printing schedule.
  • Branch Principals sign the receipt for question papers received from the System Coordinator.
  • Any shortfall or damage to printed papers must be reported immediately to the System Coordinator.
Confidentiality of question papers is non-negotiable. Any leakage — whether intentional or due to negligence — will result in disciplinary action including possible termination.
