CSM Updation

The CSM (Central School Management) portal must be fully updated before the first examination day of each term. The IT Teacher is responsible for coordinating this process.

Deadline

All CSM data must be updated and verified by 3:00 PM on the working day before the first examination.

What Must Be Updated

# Data Item Notes
1 Attendance All attendance records up to date for the term
2 Height & Weight Current measurements for all students
3 Subject Marks — CWA Class Work Assessment marks for all subjects
4 Subject Marks — RT Regular Test marks for all subjects
5 Co-Scholastic Marks Grades for co-curricular and life-skills areas
6 Student Remarks Teacher remarks for the term, reviewed and approved by the Principal

Report Card Generation

After the last examination of the term, report cards must be generated and ready by the last working day of the term. See Report Card Printing for the full printing and distribution process.

The IT Teacher must cross-check CSM data against the physical registers maintained at each branch before the deadline. Any discrepancy must be escalated to the Principal on the same day.
