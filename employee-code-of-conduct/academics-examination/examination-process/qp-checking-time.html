QP Checking Time

The number of days permitted for checking answer sheets is determined by the number of students whose papers a teacher must correct. This ensures fair timelines across branches of different sizes.

Checking Time Allocation

Number of Students Days Allowed for Checking
1 – 35 students 1 day
36 – 70 students 2 days

Important Notes

  • The checking period begins the day after the examination for that subject.
  • Answer sheets not returned within the permitted window will attract fines as per the Fine Norms.
  • Teachers must complete checking, totalling, and initialling within the allocated period — super-checking by the HOD happens after this.
  • If a teacher is absent during the checking window, the Principal must arrange an alternate checker immediately and report the situation to the System Coordinator.
