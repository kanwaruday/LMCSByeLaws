Rectification Sheet

A Rectification Sheet must be filled for every mark change identified during super-checking. This document serves as the official record of all mark corrections and must be signed by the Principal before any update is made in the CSM portal.

Standard Template

Branch Subject Date of Exam Checked By Super-Checked By S.No. Roll No. Class Student Name Old Marks New Marks Total Increased Reason for Change
Rows filled individually for each mark change

Field Explanations

Field Description
Branch Name of the LMCS branch (e.g., LMS 1, LMS 3)
Subject Name of the subject whose answer sheet is being rectified
Date of Exam Date on which the examination was conducted
Checked By Name and signature of the teacher who performed the initial marking
Super-Checked By Name and signature of the HOD or senior teacher who performed super-checking
Old Marks The marks as originally awarded by the checking teacher
New Marks The revised marks after super-checking
Total Increased Difference between new marks and old marks (positive value only; decreases are also recorded but noted separately)
Reason for Change Brief explanation: e.g., "Marking error — Question 3b answer correct but marked wrong", "Totalling error", "Missed sub-question"
Mandatory: No CSM portal update may be made without the Principal's signature on the Rectification Sheet. The IT Teacher must retain a copy of every signed Rectification Sheet for audit purposes.
