Invigilation Duty

All teachers are required to perform invigilation duty during examination periods. The rules below govern minimum hours and special exemptions.

Minimum Invigilation Requirement

Every teacher must invigilate for a minimum of 2.5 hours per examination day.

Reduced Invigilation Allowance

Teachers whose own subject examinations concluded in the past 2 days may be assigned a reduced invigilation window, at the Principal's discretion, to allow sufficient time for answer sheet checking.

  • This exception applies only when the teacher's answer-sheet checking workload directly conflicts with invigilation duty.
  • The Principal must record this in the duty register and inform the System Coordinator.
  • The teacher must still be available on campus for the full school day.

Invigilation Responsibilities

  • Invigilators must be in the examination hall before 8:45 AM to verify seating arrangements.
  • No student is permitted entry after 9:30 AM; the invigilator must enforce this strictly.
  • Mobile phones are strictly prohibited inside the examination hall for both students and invigilators.
  • Invigilators must maintain a strict silence environment and circulate continuously during the examination.
  • Any instance of malpractice must be immediately reported to the Principal and documented.
