Notebook Allocation for Academic Year 2024–25

Notebook Allocation 2024

General Instructions

  • Notebooks must be procured by parents through the school stationery list issued at the beginning of the academic year.
  • All notebooks must be covered with brown paper and labelled with the student's name, class, section and subject.
  • Subject teachers are responsible for ensuring students use the correct notebook type. Mixed-type notebooks are not permitted.
  • The school may issue notebooks directly to students through the school stationery store. In such cases, the cost is included in the fee or billed separately.
  • Notebooks must be maintained neatly. Torn, damaged or excessively dirty notebooks must be replaced by the student.

Class Teacher Responsibility

Class teachers must verify notebook compliance during the first week of the new academic year and report any discrepancies to the Principal. Subject teachers must check notebooks during the first class of each new term.
