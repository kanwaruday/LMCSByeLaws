Examination Blueprints for Academic Year 2024–25

Blueprints 2024

This Office Order prescribes the examination blueprints (marks distribution by question type and topic) for all class levels from Montessori 2 through Class XII for the academic year 2024–25. All question papers must conform to these blueprints.

M2 & M3 (Montessori 2 & 3)

Montessori level assessments are activity-based and oral. No formal written examination blueprints apply. Assessment is recorded by the Class Teacher using the Observation Record maintained in the TRR.

Practical components (20 marks) are evaluated separately from the theory paper (80 marks) as per HPBSE/CBSE guidelines.

Class XI – XII Blueprint

Class XI–XII examinations follow the HPBSE / CBSE examination pattern. Internal blueprints for school-level assessments (unit tests, half-yearly) align with the board's specified marks distribution for each subject stream. Subject teachers must obtain the latest blueprint from the Board guidelines at the beginning of each session and submit a copy to the Principal.

General Blueprint Rules

  • All question papers must strictly follow the prescribed blueprint. Deviations require prior written approval from MD Academics.
  • Marks distribution must be printed on the question paper itself for reference by students and invigilators.
  • Blueprint compliance is verified by the Principal before question papers are sent for printing.
  • For subjects with both theory and practical components, separate answer sheets are used where prescribed by the Board.
  • Any modification to the blueprint due to syllabus revision must be communicated by MD Academics via Office Order.
