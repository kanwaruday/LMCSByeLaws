Inter-School Competitions — General Guidelines

La Montessori Chain of Schools conducts regular Inter-School Competitions including Sports, Extra-Curriculars, Academic-Oriented Competitions, and others. The following guidelines must be followed during all such competitions.

  1. Principal and a concerned teacher should travel with the students. A maximum of one additional teacher can be permitted, provided the Principal sees the need.
  2. Teachers must accompany and stay with their own students throughout the competition. No separate space is to be created for teachers/staff.
  3. Participants should be accompanied by House Appointees. In special cases, the senior-most class may also be included.
  4. No transport fee shall be charged for students representing their respective schools.
  5. Students are expected to carry their own lunch boxes and water bottles.
  6. Students must cheer for their school to the best of their abilities.
  7. Host School must welcome Guest Schools to the best of their abilities.
  8. Both Host and Guest Schools are expected to take care of photography.
  9. Host School must clarify the rules before the competition.
  10. Fixtures and timelines should be shared by the Host School 2 days in advance.
  11. Punctuality must be followed; defaulting teams should be penalised.
  12. Students must be encouraged to make placards to support their respective schools.
  13. Each school should nominate commentators for the competitions.
