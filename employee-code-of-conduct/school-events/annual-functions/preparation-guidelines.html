A. Preparation Guidelines & Committees

Preparation Guidelines

  1. Each class should prepare at least 1 performance. Teachers are discouraged from merging classes.
  2. Class performances should be 5–7 minutes each.
  3. The Annual Function may or may not have a specific theme. Each school must fulfil the performance quota below.

Annual Function Organising Committee

A centralised committee is responsible for organising each aspect of the Annual Function:

  • Principal: Overall planning and management of the entire Annual Function
  • Activity Teacher: Decoration Incharge; helps the Principal track performance preparation status; curates achievement lists, alumni achievements, and School Topper Felicitation lists
  • IT Teacher: All computer and technical tasks; Annual Function PPTs; backdrop review; syncing Annual Report PPT with Principal's speech; consolidation of audios, videos, and PPTs; formulation of schedules; any tasks allocated by the Principal
  • PTI: Discipline Incharge; venue setup; coordination between teachers, clerks, and outside vendors
  • Clerk cum PRO: Food management; collection of Annual Function fees; expense consolidation; all external official tasks
