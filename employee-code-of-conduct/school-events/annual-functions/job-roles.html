E. Job Roles for Annual Functions

Principal

  • Plan and manage the entire Annual Function
  • Approve theme, event flow, timelines, and final sequence
  • Oversee rehearsals, give speech and vote of thanks
  • Prepare and present the Annual Report: a speech plus presentation of school achievements
  • Include a prize and awards list to assist management in distribution

IT Teacher

  • Prepare Annual Reports PPT and sync with the Annual Speech
  • Create the Item Flow for the Annual Function
  • Help teachers with backdrops
  • Check quality of sound for performances
  • Organise all materials for the Annual Function
  • On the day: coordinate sound, backdrop, and student entries while seated with the Sound System team

All Class Teachers

  • Prepare their respective class performances and finalise dresses
  • Create a backdrop for the performance for the Annual Function screen
  • Guide students about all school rules

Non-Class Teachers

  • All tasks assigned by the Principal

Activity Teacher

  • Award Distribution Lists
  • Board Topper Coordination for Felicitation
  • Alumni coordination for Felicitation
  • Weekly round for Performance Preparation status
  • Create a team for decoration

PTI

  • Venue preparation
  • Dresses coordination and record
  • Discipline management

Clerk cum PRO

  • Collect fees and consolidate expenses
  • Coordinate with Light, Sound, LED, Tent, and Food vendors
  • Send official letters and invites to various officials

English TGT/PGT

  • Train the compering students for better speech delivery
