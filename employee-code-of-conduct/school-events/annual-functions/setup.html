F. Annual Function Setup

A duty roster must be prepared for all teachers and staff covering the following areas:

  • Entry and Exit: Fee Clerk with assistance of Peon cum Drivers must attend entry and exit of school
  • Student Area Discipline: Duties allocated for discipline in all student seating and waiting areas
  • Green House Travel: Duties allocated for travel between the seating area and the Green House
  • Student Dispersal: Duties for teachers and helpers at places where parents receive their students
  • Corridor Discipline: Duties allocated for corridor discipline during the AF
  • Transport Management: Duties allocated for managing transport on the day
  • Food Area: Duties allocated for the food service area
