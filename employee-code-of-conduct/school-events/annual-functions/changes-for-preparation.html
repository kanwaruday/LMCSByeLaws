D. Changes at School for Preparation

Zero Period for Preparation

  • Starting 30 working days before the Annual Function, a daily Zero Period of one hour is allocated for preparation
  • All classes prepare individually
  • Participation in the Annual Function is mandatory for all students
  • Students pay as per the information provided by School Management

Classroom Setup

The classroom must be set up in a "U" formation to ensure effective practice areas during preparation.

Compering Team

  • A compering team must be set up with the English TGT/PGT as the incharge
  • Students from the Editorial Prefects or any student with a good English accent can be included
  • The compering team must include at least one student each from Class 8 and above
