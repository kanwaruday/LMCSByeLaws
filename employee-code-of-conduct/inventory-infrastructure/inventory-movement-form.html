Inventory Movement Form

Inventory Movement Form

Purpose

  • Purpose: A standardised document for recording and tracking the transfer of items within the organisation.
  • Content: Includes item descriptions, quantities, sender and receiver information, dates, and additional notes.
  • Verification: Both sender and receiver use the form to ensure a clear and verified list of transferred items, maintaining accurate inventory records.
  • Comments: Space is provided for special notes pertaining to the shipment.

Instructions for Filling the Form

Header Details

  1. Form No. — Enter the unique identification number assigned to this Movement Form.
  2. Date — Enter the date when the Movement Form is being created.

Sender Details

  1. Name — Full name of the person sending the items.
  2. Branch — Branch or location from where the items are being sent.
  3. Contact Number — Contact number of the sender for any follow-up.

Item Details

FieldDescription
Sr. No.Serial number of the item in the list
Item DescriptionBrief description of the item
QuantityNumber of units of the item being sent

Notes / Comments

Include any additional information, special instructions, or comments regarding the items or the shipment process.

Declaration

A statement confirming that the items listed have been cross-checked and verified for accuracy and condition. The sender must review and ensure all items are correctly listed and in satisfactory condition.

Receiver Details

  1. Receiver's Name — Full name of the person receiving the items.
  2. Branch — Branch or location where the items are being received.
  3. Contact Number — Contact number of the receiver for confirmation.
  4. Signature & Date — Receiver's signature confirming receipt of the items in satisfactory condition, along with the date of receipt.
