Inventory & Infrastructure

Use of School Infrastructure & Conveyance

  • The employees of the School are allowed to use the Infrastructure of the School for the overall benefit of the School. The priority for the right of usage shall be exercised by the Students first.
  • Employees are personally responsible for the maintenance and upkeep of the School Infrastructure.
  • Employees are also allowed to use the School conveyance facility in case there are vacant seats. The first priority for the right of access to School Conveyance shall be exercised by the students.
  • In case the School Conveyance is fully occupied by the students, the employee shall be expected to make arrangements for their own travel.
