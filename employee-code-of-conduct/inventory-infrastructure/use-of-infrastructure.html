Use of School Infrastructure & Conveyance

School Infrastructure

  • Employees of the School are allowed to use the School's infrastructure for the overall benefit of the School.
  • The priority for the right of usage shall be exercised by the students first.
  • Employees are personally responsible for the maintenance and upkeep of the School infrastructure they use.

School Conveyance

  • Employees are allowed to use the School conveyance facility only when there are vacant seats.
  • The first priority for access to School conveyance shall be exercised by the students.
  • If the School conveyance is fully occupied by students, the employee shall be expected to make their own travel arrangements.
