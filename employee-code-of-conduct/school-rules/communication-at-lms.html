Communication at LMS

Verbal / Phone Communication

  • This form of communication should be avoided for official purposes.
  • It is hard to keep track of and can lead to communication gaps and miscommunications.
  • As the easiest form of communication, employees must ensure that any conversation over this mode cannot be quoted as official instruction.

TPRs, Circular Registers & Other Reporting Mediums

  • This shall be the most used mode of official communication.
  • Each and every thing of significance shall be mentioned to the immediate superior through this medium.
  • This should also be used to collect and store all forms of data that the employee could need at any given point of time.

WhatsApp / SMS / Instant Messaging

  • Can be used for one-sided mass communication and announcements.
  • Must be used for coordination amongst the various employee groups.
  • Can also be used by an employee to escalate beyond the immediate superior when necessary.
  • Particularly useful for out-of-office hours communication.

Emails / Official Letters / Applications

  • This form of communication must be used for very important information and formal requests.
  • All emails aimed at coordinators and MDs should be sent to the official school email address.
  • In case of any non-academic communication or formal request, this medium must be used.
