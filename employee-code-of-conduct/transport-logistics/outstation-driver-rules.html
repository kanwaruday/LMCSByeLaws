Outstation Driver Rules

It has been decided by the Management that the following will be the terms and conditions for outstation drivers. Outstation Duty is one that does not include a visit to one of the Schools.

Day Trip (No Overnight Stay)

  1. The driver will be entitled to Rs. 200/- for the day for all sorts of expenses.
  2. The driver will also be entitled to claim fare of bus travel, subject to the submission of Bus Tickets.

Overnight Outstation Duty

  1. The driver is entitled to Rs. 500/- per day for all personal expenses including food etc.
  2. If the tour includes bus travel, the driver will be entitled to claim the fare, subject to submission of Bus Tickets.
  3. In a case where a driver is on outstation duty and the School of the driver is closed (Off-Day), the driver is entitled to 1 Compensatory Leave per Off-Day at School.
  4. The driver is entitled to 1 Compensatory Leave after each tour.
