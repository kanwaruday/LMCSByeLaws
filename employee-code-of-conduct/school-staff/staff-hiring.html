Staff Hiring

Once a candidate receives MD Academics' approval, the following onboarding steps must be completed before the staff member begins work.

Hiring Form

A formal Hiring Form is filled for every new appointment. This form captures the final agreed designation, branch assignment, salary, joining date and any special conditions. The form is signed by the appointing authority and filed in the staff record.

Appointment Letter

An official Appointment Letter is issued to the candidate on LMCS letterhead. The letter specifies:

  • Designation and reporting structure
  • Branch and grade/subject assignment
  • Gross monthly salary and components
  • Probation period (typically 3 months)
  • Terms of service, notice period and conditions of employment

The appointment letter must be signed and returned by the candidate before the joining date.

Document Collection

The following documents are collected from every new staff member at the time of joining:

  • Original educational qualification certificates (verified and returned)
  • TET certificate (if applicable)
  • Previous employment experience letters
  • Aadhar Card / Government ID proof
  • PAN Card (for salary tax purposes)
  • Bank account details for salary disbursement
  • Passport-size photographs (4 copies)
  • Medical fitness certificate (for new teaching staff)

All documents are filed in the staff personal dossier maintained at the branch and a copy is forwarded to the Head Office HR file.

Job Roles Document

Every staff member is given the official Job Roles document for their designation. The document outlines day-to-day responsibilities, reporting lines, performance expectations and KPIs. The staff member acknowledges receipt of the Job Roles document in writing.

Service Contract

A Service Contract is signed between LMCS and the new employee covering the terms and duration of service, intellectual property, confidentiality and non-compete obligations. The contract is retained in the staff file.

Official Email ID

An official LMCS email ID is created for every new staff member. The email ID follows the format firstname.lastname@lmcs.edu.in. All official school communication must be conducted via the official email ID only — personal email accounts must not be used for school business.

WhatsApp & Communication Groups

New staff are added to the relevant WhatsApp groups for their branch and subject/department. Access to school communication channels is through the registered personal mobile number of the staff member. Staff must not share group content outside the group.

School Mobile App

Teaching and administrative staff are required to install and log in to the official LMCS school management mobile app. App credentials are issued by the IT/Admin coordinator. The app is used for attendance marking, report card access and parent communication.

Social Media Guidelines

Staff members must not post, share or publish any school-related content on personal social media accounts without prior written approval from the Principal or MD. This includes photographs of students, classrooms, exam papers, internal notices and school events. Violation of the social media policy may result in disciplinary action.

Induction Scheduling

An induction session is scheduled for all new staff within the first week of joining. The Principal coordinates with MD Academics to fix the induction date. Details of the induction programme are covered in the Staff Induction section.
