Class 11 Taster Classes 2024 — LMS Kelheli

Taster Classes 2024

LMS Kelheli will be starting its Class 11 at its campus. Below is the plan of action for the Classes:

  1. Classes Begin: 8 April 2024
  2. Round 1 — Compulsory Taster Classes: 8 April to 19 April 2024 — teachers introduce their respective subject along with an overview of the scope of their subjects.

Round 1 Taster Class Schedule (8–19 April 2024)

DateSubjects
8 April 2024Physics & History
9 April 2024English & Physical Education
10 April 2024Chemistry & Accountancy
12 April 2024Economics & Political Science
18 April 2024Mathematics & Hindi
19 April 2024Business Studies & Biology
  1. A Career Counselling Session will be held for all students on 20 April 2024.
  2. Round 2 — Subject Understanding Classes: 22 and 23 April 2024 — students will be made aware of the entire course to be taught in Classes 11 & 12, along with subject weightages.
  3. Teaching Classes begin: 24 April 2024.

On the basis of the Career Counselling Assessment, 1-on-1 Career Counselling Sessions will be conducted to guide all students on their choice of stream and their preferred preparation for Competitive Examination for College Entry.
