Revised Transport Fee Structure – Post COVID

Transport Fee 2021

After the COVID-19 pandemic and the worldwide lockdown, the Schools have begun to open and so shall the transport facility. Key context:

  • The pending taxes and other liabilities on the School Buses are huge and the School received no Transport Fees in 2020.
  • The School is being requested to resume the transport service and shall do so.
  • Schools will initially start with the buses that require the minimum expense to resume operations.
  • The School shall take assurance from parents before starting any particular route.

Phase Rules

  • Phase 1 applies until the Government gives tax relief — latest until the end of the First Term.
  • Phase 2 applies either upon receiving relief from the Government or from the Second Term onwards.
  • Routes are finalised based on feasibility from the parent survey.
