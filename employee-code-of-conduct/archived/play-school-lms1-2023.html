Starting of Play School at La Montessori School Dhalpur Kullu (LMS 1)

Play School @ LMS 1 – 2023

The School management is pleased to announce the starting of the Play School at La Montessori School Dhalpur Kullu. The following are the key points:

  1. Students above the age of 2 are to be targeted so that proper advice can be given to the prospective parents.
  2. In the month of October, students are expected to attend the School from 10 am to 11 am.
  3. The Classes for the rest of the year would be held from 10 am to 1 pm.
  4. New admission fees, admission form fees and security will be charged to enrol the student.
  5. 50% Discount on New Admission Fees for LMS Alumni.
  6. No Tuition Fees will be charged till 31 March 2024.
  7. A separate teacher shall be spared to conduct the said Classes and the MD Academics will be instrumental in guiding the teacher for the detailed instructions and plans.
