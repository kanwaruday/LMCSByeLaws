Preparation Tasks for Heads — January 2024

Preparation for 2024 Session – Heads

As we prepare for the upcoming weeks, the following crucial tasks are expected from the various Heads. Adhering to specified timelines is essential to ensure smooth functioning and organisation of school activities.

  1. Poster Details Submission — Due: Friday, 19th Jan 2024
    Heads are requested to share the details of posters pasting (via their respective clerks) by this Friday. Please mention the overall posters received and the number of posters pasted.
  2. New Admission Details Submission
    Heads are requested to share new admission details on a daily basis w.e.f. 22nd January 2024 with the assistance of their respective clerks.
  3. Staffing Requirement and New Appointments — Deadline: 27th Jan 2024
    All new appointments should be finalised by 27th Jan 2024. Non-availability of appropriate staff by 27th January 2024 will be treated with utmost seriousness.
  4. School Requirement List Submission — Deadline: 5th February 2024
    Heads of each school are requested to compile and share the School requirement list by 5th February 2024. This list should cover any essential items or resources needed for smooth operation.
  5. Sports Trials & CBSE Cluster Games Details — Deadline: 27th January 2024
    The Head in collaboration with PTIs is tasked with gathering and sharing the details of upcoming sports trials and CBSE Cluster Games by 27th January 2024, so that an appropriate plan of action can be formulated.
