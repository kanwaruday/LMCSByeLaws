Foundation Classes for Montessori 2 – 2019

Foundation Classes 2018

The Management has decided to start Foundation Classes for Montessori 2 of 2019 w.e.f. 11th October 2018. Following are the steps involved for the said classes:

1. Spreading the Word for Foundation Classes

  1. The Office Clerk / Admin Staff of each School will receive pamphlets for School Almanacs to be pasted from Classes M-3 to Class 8.
  2. The Office Clerk in consultation with the respective Principal must allocate appropriate staff to paste the said pamphlets in the School Almanac.
  3. The Class Teachers are supposed to ensure that the pamphlets are pasted in all of the School Almanacs.
NB: Office Clerks are requested to report compliance over the "LMS Admins" WhatsApp Group.

2. Operation of the Foundation Classes

  1. The Foundation Classes will be merged with the existing Montessori 1.
  2. The Foundation Classes will emphasise on the following:
    • Acclimatization to the School Environment for Montessori 2 of Academic Session 2019–2020
    • Listening, Speaking & Reading Skill
    • Conceptual Development with Montessori Equipment
NB: Concerned Principals are requested to clear doubts with the M.D. Academics.
