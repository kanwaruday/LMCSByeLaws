Class 11 Reopening & Taster Classes — LMS Kelheli

Taster Classes 2023 – LMS 2 Class 11

LMS Kelheli has decided to reopen Classes 11 & 12 and in this academic year, LMS Kelheli will be opening Class 11 at its campus. Below is the plan of action:

  1. Admissions Begin: 23 March 2023
  2. Classes Begin: 17 April 2023
  3. Compulsory Taster Classes: 17 April to 22 April 2023 — teachers introduce their respective subject along with an overview of the expected course for the next 2 years.

Taster Class Schedule (17–22 April 2023)

22 April 2023Business Studies & Biology
  1. An optional Career Counselling Assessment will be conducted for all students on 24 April 2023.
  2. Teaching Classes begin: 24 April 2023.
  3. On the basis of the Career Counselling Assessment, 1-on-1 Career Counselling Sessions will be conducted to guide all students on their choice of stream and preferred preparation for Competitive Examination for College Entry.
