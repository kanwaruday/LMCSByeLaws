Schedule & Agenda — 27 January 2024

Combined Meeting Agenda – January 2024

Schedule for 27 January 2024

  • Half-day mode from 9 am to 1 pm.
  • Class 10 doubt-clearing session from 9 am to 1 pm (subject-wise) — ITs to work on the timetable.
  • Teachers' Meeting from 10 am to 1 pm.
  • Pre-board examination timing: 9 am to 12 noon, with one hour dedicated to games organised by the PTIs.
  • 29 January 2024 onwards — Classes from 9 am to 3 pm; regular classes commence.

Agenda for Teachers' Meeting (27 January 2024)

To be conducted by the respective Principals:

  1. New admission status — enquire about the efforts put in by each teacher.
  2. Gather information about competitor schools and new things they are doing.
  3. Ensure Revision Tests (RTs) are taken as per the schedule and Course Mapping.
  4. Update extracurricular activities, remarks, marks and grades on the CSM Portal.
  5. Update Teachers' TRR work.
  6. Special emphasis on Practicals and Classroom activities. Take suggestions from teachers to plan for Activity Days (Mylestone Days).
  7. Share daily plans until the commencement of the annual papers.
  8. Annual Functions:
    • LMS 1, 2, 3 — discuss the theme for Annual Function 2024 and start thinking of performances.
    • LMS 4 — focus on preparations for the Annual Function scheduled for the first week of February.
  9. Graduation ceremony of MW.

Work to be Updated by the Principal

  1. Assess the status of teachers and helpers regarding their continuity for the next session.
  2. Work on Period Allotment ensuring sufficient games periods in all classes.
  3. Review report cards with the assistance of IT teachers.
  4. Update all School Registers.
  5. Discuss the rules of "Seven Stones" (pithoo) with the PTI so that finalised information can be updated by the afternoon of January 27.
