Guidelines for the Free Play School

Play School 2024

Class Assignment

New admissions will be enrolled in the M-1 class, focusing on helping new students acclimatize to the school environment so they get comfortable staying away from their parents.

Fee Structure

  • A one-time admission fee, admission form fee and security deposit will apply.
  • Tuition fees are fully waived until 31 March 2025.

Class Timings (Phased Schedule)

WeekTiming
First week10:00 am – 11:00 am
Second week10:00 am – 12:00 pm
Third week onwards10:00 am – 1:00 pm

Transportation

New students are eligible for free bus service until the new session begins.

Warm Welcome

New students should be warmly welcomed with a tikka ceremony, receive badges (Smilies / "My First Day at School" etc.) and have commemorative photos taken to mark their start.

Classroom Presentation

Principals are requested to submit a high-quality video of their M-1 classroom. If any tidying up is needed, work with teachers to make the space as inviting and engaging as possible.

Admission Criteria

Admission efforts should focus on children over the age of two:

ClassDate of Birth later than
M-131st March 2022
M-231st March 2021
M-331st March 2020
C-131st March 2019
C-231st March 2018

School Uniform

Parents are not required to purchase a school uniform for play school students. Uniforms will be applicable only from the start of the new session.

Student Essentials

Parents are requested to provide: a bag, handkerchief, water bottle and a home-packed lunch — each labelled with the student's name. No book sets are required.
