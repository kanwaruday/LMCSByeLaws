COVID Online Classes – Plan of Action

COVID Online Classes 2020

In wake of the Nationwide Corona Virus Lockdown announced by the Honourable Prime Minister on the evening of 24th March 2020, La Montessori Schools have decided to test & prepare for Online Classes to be conducted for the students of La Montessori Chain of Schools.

26th March 2020

Computer Teachers

  1. Sort out the timetable to be followed by the teachers w.e.f. 1 April 2020:
    • Must have all main periods every day — English, Hindi, Mathematics, Science/EVS, Social Sciences, Sanskrit.
    • 40-minute periods, with 5-minute buffer time & 5-minute settling down time.
    • Can also have back-to-back classes as no daily notebook checking.
  2. Help School Teachers & Clerks cum PRO to log on to the Video Conferencing Tool for the evening meeting to be held at 3 pm.
  3. Confirm about the correctness & 100% enrollment of all of the students to the Mailing Lists with Clerks cum PRO.

All Teachers

  1. Join the Video Conference at least once before 2 pm.
  2. Attend the 3 pm Combined Teachers Meeting held through the Video Conferencing Tool.

Clerk cum PRO

  • Assist Computer Teachers in any kind of need.

27th–31st March 2020

Computer Teachers

  1. Prepare Peer Teacher Workshop Group Video Conference Links.
  2. Ensure that the Video Conference happens smoothly.
  3. Make Class Groups as per discussion; use Nomenclature as "LMS x – Class x".

All Teachers

  1. Prepare for a Peer Teacher Workshop on video conference.
  2. Can teach a topic of choice.

Principals

  1. Give demos for Peer Teacher Workshop.
  2. Observe other teachers and give constructive feedback.

Clerk cum PRO

  • Assist Computer Teachers in any kind of need.
Important: Announce about Online Classes to Parents & request them to activate the system!

28th–31st March 2020 — Parent Onboarding

Computer Teachers

  1. Help Parents with getting used to Emails & Video Conferencing Tool.
  2. Request Parents to activate the Video Conference at least once so that the Classes can begin from 1 April 2020.
  3. Mark attendance of Parents who have been able to activate the video conference.

Fee Clerks cum PRO

  1. Send SMS to Parents who have still not activated — 2 times a day.
  2. All parents who have not activated the Video Conferencing yet should be telephonically called on 30 March 2020 & 31 March 2020.
