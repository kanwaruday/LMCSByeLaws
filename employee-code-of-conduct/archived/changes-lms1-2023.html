Proposed Changes at LMS 1 following Teacher Meetings & Feedback

Changes at LMS 1 – 2023

After a series of meetings and feedback sessions with the Teachers at LMS 1, the following changes were proposed:

New Teacher Induction Timeline

Previously, new teachers were expected to perform as full-fledged teachers from Day 1. The revised induction timeline is:

PhaseDuties
Day 1 onwards Classroom Teaching, Notebook Checking, Class Attendance, TRR Updation, HW/CWA on Emails, WhatsApp Involvement, Google Space Involvement
Week 3 onwards Preparing CWA along with Solutions; Class Teacher Duties (Records in TRR)
Month 2 onwards Get involved in all School Activities; can be appointed as House Moderator; CSM Marks Updation
  • Within 1 week of joining, an Induction Meeting is conducted with the Counter Teacher, Principal and Academic Coordinator.
  • In the first 2 weeks, the CWA along with solutions will be provided by the Counter Teacher.
  • For the first 2 months, all Support Sessions will be conducted by Peer Teachers. Third month onwards, sessions are conducted by the Principal & Academic Coordinator.
  • If a teacher has joined less than 2 months before an upcoming Semester Examination, they are exempted from making the question paper (provided the Counter Teacher is experienced).
  • For the first examination question paper, the error sheet must still be generated and fine calculated — but a new teacher is exempted from paying the fine.

Discipline at LMS

  1. Teachers should be asked to only speak in English. If by 30th September 2023, teachers are unable to comply, a fine system will be implemented.
  2. Students should be encouraged to speak in English from MW to SW.
  3. Principal, Teachers, PTI, IT Teacher, Helpers and Drivers must all ensure uniform tidiness and English speaking.
  4. Student fines should be restarted for English Speaking, Hair, Nails, Uniforms, Late Fine etc.
  5. Students should be made to write their HW in the School Almanac.
  6. No HW to be sent on Emails; Daily Plan still on Google Spaces.
  7. Uniform Days — Monday, Wednesday, Friday. Track Suits Days — Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday.
  8. Parents should be allowed in School Premises only when invited.
  9. Before each period, teachers must remind students about their School Uniform tidiness.
  10. Games Periods can be cancelled to ensure discipline; a teacher can also give a Games Period to reward a class (teacher must accompany the class; Principal must be informed in TRR).
  11. All fine money to be used by Principal to provide various resources.

Activities and Academics

  1. SOPs for activities should only be generated for an activity with 3 or more participating Classes/Teachers.
  2. Ideally only 1 SOP Event per month — not exceeding 10 events in an academic year (inclusive of Investiture Day, Annual Function, 3 Showcase Days, Activity Day, Inter-LMS Competitions, Fetes on Open House Days).
  3. All Festival Celebrations & Special Days to be conducted by House Appointees during Morning Assemblies.
  4. Each Mylestone Activity needs to be conducted in the class with pictures clicked by the conducting teacher.
  5. Remedial Classes: subject-wise allocation of days; 2 students per class recommended by IT Teacher based on CWA scores; recommended by teacher for upcoming month.
  6. No Weekly Open House — only Morning MOH at the end of the months.

Other Changes

  1. CWA Solutions can be handwritten or typed based on teacher preference (untidy work will require typing).
  2. Fines on Teachers for non-submission of CWAs.
  3. All fines will be reflected on Salary Slips.
