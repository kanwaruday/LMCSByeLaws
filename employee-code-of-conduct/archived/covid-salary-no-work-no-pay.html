COVID – Salary Rule: No Work, No Pay

This is in reference to the directions issued by the Secretary Education to Government of H.P. to the Director Higher Education & Director Elementary Education dated 30th October 2020. With effect from the salary of November 2020, the following will be applicable:

  1. The working employees — Teachers and Admin Staff — shall continue to receive the salary as per the existing practice.
  2. Being employees of LMS, the Helpers and Peon cum Drivers shall be paid the salary for all Sundays and 1 second Saturday, i.e. 5 days.
  3. Beyond point 2, the employees will be paid based on their attendance at the School on a "No Work, No Pay" basis.
