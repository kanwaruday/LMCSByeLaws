Incentives & Bonuses

The following incentive and bonus schemes are available to employees at La Montessori Schools:

🎓

Students New Admission Incentive

Incentive awarded to staff for successfully bringing in new student admissions

⭐

Performance Bonus

Bonus awarded based on evaluated academic and professional performance

📈

System of Increments

Annual salary increment structure tied to years of service and performance

👨‍👩‍👧

Concession for Staff Children

Fee concession scheme for children of school employees

Detailed scheme parameters are maintained by the Head Office. Contact your Principal or the HR department for eligibility and current rates.
