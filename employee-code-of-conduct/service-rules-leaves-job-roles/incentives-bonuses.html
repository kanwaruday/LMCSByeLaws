Incentives & Bonuses

LMCS offers structured incentive and bonus schemes to reward employee performance, referrals, and loyalty.

🎓

New Admission Incentive

OO/02/26 — Employee referral: 10% incentive + 20% parent discount; branch-wise table
🏆

Performance Bonus

Bi-annual bonus split: ½ fixed equally + ½ performance-based on daily reporting scores
📈

System of Increments

Annual 3% increment in September + DA changes; 3-year lock-in for new joinees from Jan 2020
👨‍👩‍👧

Concession for Staff Children

OO/06/23 — Admission, tuition, and annual fees waived for children of all staff members
