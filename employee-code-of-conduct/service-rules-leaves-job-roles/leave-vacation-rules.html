Leave & Vacation Rules

Leave entitlements at La Montessori Schools are organised into structured categories. Leave is a privilege, not a right, and all leave must be applied for in advance as per the rules below.

📋

General Leave Rules

11 foundational rules — advance notice, responsibility handover, sandwiched leaves
🎁

Leave Benefits (CL & Comp.)

Casual Leave (1/month teaching, ½ vacation days admin) and Compensatory Leave rules
📅

Day Leaves

Single Leave (unpaid), Double Leave (2-day limit), and Absent (2-day deduction)
⏱️

Time-Based Leaves

Late Leave (4=1 day), Short Leave (3=1 day), Half Leave (2=1 day)
⭐

Special & Work-Based Leave

Special Day Leave (2 days), Special Day Absent (5 days), Non-Compliance Leave (−0.1/instance)
🏖️

Non-Teaching Staff Vacations

OO/05/21 — ½ working vacation days = CL for admin/support staff
