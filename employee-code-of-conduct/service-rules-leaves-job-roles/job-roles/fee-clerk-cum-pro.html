Fee Clerk cum PRO

Usual working timings: 8:45 AM to 5:00 PM. Expected working days per year: 265 to 290 days.

Fee Collection Duties

  • Collect fees from all students between prescribed times as per school rules.
  • Generate a report of fees collected on a particular day at the end of the day and present it to the financial head of the institution.
  • Deposit fees collected on a particular day in the bank within 24 hours of collection.
  • Obtain the financial head's signature in the ledger after showing proof of bank transaction via net banking.
  • Check emails for administrative-related school works like NOC, last date for External Activities (EA), affiliation authority.
  • Keep check on all day-to-day money matters: workshops, donations, fines, examination expenses, etc.
  • Log all expenses into the Physical Day Book at the counter and on the Day Book Google Sheet every day.
  • Collect an article from the Editorial Bench of the school after each event and submit it to the press reporter.
  • Maintain all student records (student details, CCE mark updation, etc.) and report to the Principal and concerned authority.

Administrative Duties (Working Days)

  • Check emails for administrative-related school work: NOC, EA deadlines, affiliation, CCE mark updation.
  • Maintain student details, parent details and contact information, marks, fees information, and certificates on the school software as well as in hard copy.
  • Maintain teacher records on the software as well as in files.
  • Issue various certificates: Transfer Certificate, Mark List, Migration, "To Whom It May Concern" letters, etc. Cross-check all related information including financial accounts before issuing.

Working Days & Holidays

  • One holiday per week — generally Sunday, but may be any day per school requirements.
  • All gazetted holidays of Himachal Pradesh + 2 local holidays are given off. Compensatory leave may be claimed if called on a gazetted holiday.
  • 1 Casual Leave per month (paid). In case of accumulation, leaves may be encashed at the rate of ½ day per accumulated leave.
  • National holidays are expected to be working days.
  • Expected to work for 265 to 290 days per year.
