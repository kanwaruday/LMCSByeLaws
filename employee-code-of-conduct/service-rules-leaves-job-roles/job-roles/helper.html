Helper

Reporting time: 7:45 AM

Daily Duties

  1. Reporting time is 7:45 AM.
  2. Open the school premises and do dusting and sweeping/cleaning of the gate area, offices, and other specific areas instructed by the in-charge.
  3. After cleaning, be present around the gate area to receive students, listen to parents' issues pertaining to students, and inform the superior if needed.
  4. During morning assembly, the primary duty is to help the teachers.
  5. Perform prescribed duties in the Montessori Wing during school hours, along with washroom duty.
  6. Stamping notebooks during the day is also part of a helper's duty.
  7. Helpers are responsible for the safety, well-being, and discipline of students.
  8. Helpers must exercise responsibility especially during the dispersal time of the Montessori Wing and Primary Wing.
  9. Helpers assigned to school buses must perform bus duty as the conductor of the bus.
  10. Helpers must ensure they have lunch at different times so that the majority of helpers are available to teachers and students at all given times.
  11. Helpers must clean the Montessori classrooms between 1 PM and 3 PM.
  12. Facilitate the dispersal process of the senior students.
  13. From 3 PM onwards (after school), sweep and dust all classrooms.
  14. Ensure that the water supply is regular (switch on the motor at the right times).
  15. Responsible for watering plants regularly.
  16. Must adhere to tasks given by the PTI, Fee Clerk, Principal, and Teachers.
