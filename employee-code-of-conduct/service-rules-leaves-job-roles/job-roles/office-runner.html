Office Runner (Inactive Post)

Morning Duties

  • Report to the School Administrator.
  • Open offices, gates and classrooms.
  • Prepare the school premises in accordance with the day.
  • Morning Assembly preparation.
  • Fish feed and other supervision of property.
  • Music system.
  • Set up stage.
  • Arrange benches, tables, etc.
  • Coordinate with other helping staff when items need to be transferred between schools.

During School Hours

  • Look after student discipline from the moment they enter the premises.
  • Interact with parents at the gate as required and report problems to the administrator in the evening.
  • Be responsible for the gate throughout the entire day.
  • Ring the bell at the start and end of each period.
  • Take appointments on behalf of the coordinator.
  • Manage students during recess, computer and games periods.
  • Facilitate smooth boarding of students onto buses.
  • Maintain related registers.
  • Facilitate noting down of late-comers — students and teachers.
  • Intimate parents of defaulters.
  • Get registers signed by the respective people.
  • Attend phone calls, convey correct information and record in the register.
  • Work as an office runner for executive staff (Principal, Managers, Chairman, etc.).

Infrastructure Maintenance

  • Look after water, electricity and other infrastructure of the school.
  • During holidays, get maintenance done — furniture, building repairs and painting.
  • Ensure cleanliness and arrangements for the new session.

Stationery & Logistics

  • Help maintain stationery records at the beginning of the year.
  • Be responsible for logistics required for smooth school operations — chalk, dusters, test sheets, etc.
  • Be in-charge of stationery issued to students: books, notebooks, etc.
  • Xerox rhymes for the Montessori Wing.

Examination Duties

  • Record height and weight for students.
  • Xerox examination papers for the school.
  • Arrange required stationery from the market (thread, maps, graph paper, etc.).

Miscellaneous

  • In the absence of any other support or admin staff, work on their behalf.

Timing & Working Days

  • Usual working hours: 7:45 am to 5:00 pm.
  • One holiday per week (generally Sunday, but may vary as per school requirements).
  • All Himachal Pradesh gazette holidays and 2 local holidays are given off; the employee may still be called on gazette holidays. Compensatory holiday claimable up to twice the working time, subject to approval.
  • 2 Casual Leaves per month (paid). Accumulated leaves can be encashed at the rate of ½ day per accumulated leave.
  • National holidays are expected to be working days.
  • Expected working days per year: 265 to 290 days.
