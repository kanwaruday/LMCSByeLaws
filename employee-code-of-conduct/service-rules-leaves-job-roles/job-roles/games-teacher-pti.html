Games Teacher / PTI

The PTI must be present in the school campus at least 30 minutes before and after school. School timings: 8:30 AM to 5:00 PM.

Stock Maintenance

  • Maintain all sports-related stock and equipment. Keep an up-to-date inventory.

Management of Helping Staff

  • Ensure that helping staff perform their prescribed duties.
  • Report irregularities to the Principal.
  • Organise staff for preparation of major events (Investiture, Sports Day, House Competitions, Annual Function, etc.).
  • Allot and administer helping staff before and after official school timing.

Timetable Management

  • Make timetable adjustments as per daily circumstances.
  • Be aware of all adjustments of the day and act as a centre for information in this regard.
  • Ensure teachers adhere to the timetable and discipline with the right spirit.
  • During examinations, assign examination duties and ensure smooth conduction by arranging material in advance (sheets, maps, thread, etc.).

Money Matters

  • Deal with day-to-day money matters of the school and coordinate with office for Head Office communication.

Discipline

  • Act as a right hand to the Principal in managing and coordinating the school, staff, and students.
  • Take disciplinary action against those who violate school norms.
  • Conduct continuous rounds of the school to ensure discipline and cleanliness.
  • Take care of general defaults — be strict on uniform, overall decorum, and English.
  • Counsel students who are sent out by teachers.

Physical Education

  • At least 50% of allotted periods must be dedicated to theory for Classes XI and XII.
  • Conduct fortnightly tests of theory taught.
  • Set examination papers as per the school routine.

Sports Competitions

  • Liaise with sports authorities for coordination and sports functioning.
  • Prepare sports teams for cluster games.
  • May include sports practices on holidays and early mornings before school.
