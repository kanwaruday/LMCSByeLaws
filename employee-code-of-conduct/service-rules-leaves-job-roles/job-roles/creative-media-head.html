Creative Media Head — Job Role

Creative Media Head

An additional role within the chain of Schools, initially filled by one of the existing teachers. The Creative Media Head is responsible for the school's social media presence across all platforms.

Core Job Role

  • Compile and aesthetically design posts for Social Media.
  • Generate content for Social Media.
  • Post on Social Media regularly.

YouTube

Goal: A platform for documenting videos for future nostalgia.

SOP for Content

  1. Computer Teachers collect pictures & videos of various activities from Class Teachers, Activity In-Charges, etc.
  2. Computer Teachers keep backup of pictures in organised folders.
  3. Computer Teachers regularly send 10–20 pictures/short videos to the Creative Media Head, along with the name of activity and a description.
  4. The Creative Media Head designs and executes content as per their job role.
