IT Teacher

Marks Updation

  • Check and compile CWA results on a fortnightly basis for parent-principal interaction.
  • Compile FA & SA marks at the end of the respective time period.
  • Assist teachers in the data entry process when help is required.

Computer Laboratory & Hardware

  • Maintenance of computer lab — hardware and software.
  • Maintain and troubleshoot the EPABX system installed in the school campus.
  • Look after and provide basic troubleshooting for CCTV cameras and all other hardware and software installed at the school.

Disciplinary Duty

  • Keep a check on the fine register and collection.

Digital Classes

  • Keep a check on all digital classes to ensure they are working properly.
  • Update the digital class record regularly.
  • Check the peripherals of digital classes and lab for proper working.
  • Generate a Monthly Usage Report and present it at the Monthly Meet.

Examination

  • Rate the quality of papers submitted by teachers by email.
  • Fix the formatting of papers before printing them.
  • Help in the printing of examination papers.

Social Media Responsibilities

  • Facebook: Regular status updates and at least 10 photographs per day.
  • Instagram: 1 photograph per day.
  • Twitter: Status updates.
  • YouTube: Regular videos of weekly events.
  • Website: Choose the best 20 photographs every week and send for the website; provide regular data updates.

Data Organisation

  • Methodical daily storage of data (text, videos, photos, presentations).
  • Compression of files and removal of slack files on a regular basis.

General Teaching Responsibility

The IT Teacher is responsible for the computer lab and all other IT-related jobs including digital classroom management, examination paper printing, and CBSE online updations. They must be ready to stay after school if required.

External Assessment (DA)

  • Set up the lab for DA and classes during morning assembly.
  • Make DA schedule and circulate it in time to ensure smooth conduct.
  • Undertake DA tests, giving a uniform time to all students.

Computer Classes

  • Take lab and activity classes after the end of each theoretical chapter.
  • At the end of each semester, computers must be formatted and cleaned using Collin.
  • Computers must be dusted monthly using a blower.
  • At least 25% of periods should be taken as theory classes.
  • Must stay updated with the latest developments in computers.
