Peon cum Driver

Usual working timings: 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Expected minimum working days per year: 220 days.

Bus Duties (8–9 AM, 1–2 PM, 3–4 PM)

  • Reach the bus starting point 5–10 minutes before departure for basic dusting and cleaning of the bus.
  • Inspect the bus for signs of wear and tear. The driver is responsible for the assigned bus and must look after its service, documents, and maintenance.
  • Drive at moderate speeds — no rash driving. Pick up students from allocated stops and patiently wait for students to board or alight.
  • Use polite language with appropriate distance and body language — preferably English — with parents, students, and staff.
  • Fill up the log book for the bus and attend to fuel requirements.
  • Adhere to all norms of driving.
  • In case of an accident, the driver must obtain compensation from the other party involved. Any shortfall is paid by the driver, as accidents are treated as negligence.

Duties During School Hours

  • Sit in the assigned corridor / place in the school, ensuring good discipline, and work as a helping hand to teachers and other support staff.
  • Be instrumental in day-to-day jobs at the school: gardening, moving furniture, basic dusting, facilitating games, office running, and other school-related jobs.
  • Must not refuse work assigned by the in-charge (PTI & Principal). For each refusal reported by the in-charge, a half-day's salary will be deducted.
  • Lunch must be taken individually in the bus as per the schedule agreed upon by drivers.
  • Must not use a mobile phone during school hours.
  • Be ready to work extra on special occasions.

Duties During Student Holidays

  • Be aware of all documents for their respective vehicle and update them during holidays.
  • Come to school before the beginning of session to help set up the school for re-opening.
  • Must always be on call for school / school management requirements. In case of unavailability, a leave will be deducted.

Working Days & Holidays

  • One holiday per week — generally Sunday, but may be any day per school requirements.
  • All gazetted holidays of Himachal Pradesh + 2 local holidays are given off. The employee may still be called on a gazetted holiday; compensatory leave may be claimed for up to twice the working time, subject to approval by the head of the institution.
  • Expected to be present for at least one-fourth of the duration of school vacations. The placement of working days within vacations is at the discretion of the head of the institution.
