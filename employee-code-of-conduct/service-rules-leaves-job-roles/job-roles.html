Job Roles

The following staff positions are defined at La Montessori Schools:

Teaching Staff

👩‍🏫

Teacher

Classroom teacher responsible for instruction, assessment and student development

💻

IT Teacher

Responsible for ICT instruction and managing school technology systems

🏃

Games Teacher / PTI

Physical Training Instructor responsible for sports and physical education

Leadership & Administration

🏫

Principal

Head of the school responsible for academic and administrative leadership

🎨

Creative Media Head

Responsible for school communications, media and creative content

💼

Fee Clerk cum PRO

Manages fee collection, records and public relations at the school level

Support Staff

🧹

Helper

Provides support for classroom, campus maintenance and student supervision

🚗

Peon cum Driver

Handles school transport and general peon duties

📦

Office Runner

(Inactive Post) — Previously responsible for inter-office document delivery
