Incentives & Bonuses

LMCS offers structured incentive and bonus schemes to reward employee performance, referrals, and loyalty. All schemes are governed by the rules below.

🎓

New Admission Incentive

Employee referral scheme — 10% incentive + 20% parent discount; branch-wise table
🏆

Performance Bonus

Bi-annual bonus calculated on daily reporting scores — fixed half + performance half
📈

System of Increments

Annual 3% increment, DA changes, and the 3-year no-increment rule from Jan 2020
👨‍👩‍👧

Concession for Staff Children

OO/06/23 — no admission, tuition, or annual fees for children of staff members
