Students New Admission Incentive

1. Employee Referral Scheme

If a staff member refers a new admission:

  • The new parent can receive a 20% discount on Admission Fees.
  • The referring employee receives a 10% incentive from the collected fees.
  • If the employee chooses not to offer the 20% discount to the parent, they can claim 10% of the total admission fee paid (i.e., 10% of the full, undiscounted amount).

2. Immediate Relative Discount

  • A 50% Discount on New Admission Fees is given to immediate relatives of staff members (excludes Helping Staff and Drivers).
  • "Immediate relative" means an immediate brother/sister of the employee or the spouse of the employee.
  • This does not include cousins of the employee or cousins of the employee's spouse.
  • The relationship must be confirmed by the Accounts Coordinator.
  • No employee incentive is applicable for this category.

Payment Timeline

  • The parent discount is offered at the time of payment of New Admission Fees.
  • Incentives to employees are paid after October of the present year.
