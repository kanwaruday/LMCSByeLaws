Concession for Staff Children

With effect from 1st April 2023, the following fee concessions are applicable for the wards of all school employees:

Fee Waivers

Fee Head Applicable to Staff Child?
New Admission Fees ✅ Waived — No new admission fees charged
Tuition Fees ✅ Waived — No tuition fees charged
Annual Fees ✅ Waived — No annual fees charged
Transport Fee ❌ Applicable as per norms
Co-Curricular Fees (Dance, Music, Karate) ❌ Applicable as per norms
Curricular Activity Fees (DA, OD, Conferences, Workshops) ❌ Applicable as per norms
Other Fees ❌ Applicable as per norms
This concession applies to children of all existing staff members. New staff members joining after 1st April 2023 are eligible from their date of joining. Any query regarding this order should be directed to the Accounts Coordinator.
