2. New Admission Fees Redistribution Scheme

Class Teachers of Montessori 1 & Montessori 2

  • Condition: There are 25 students in the class.
  • For each new admission above 25, the teacher receives a one-time incentive of Rs. 2,000 per new admission.
  • Example — M-2: Total Students 38 → Incentive-eligible: 38−25 = 13 → Total: Rs. 26,000

Class Teachers of Montessori 3 to Class 2

  • Condition: More than 2 new admissions.
  • For each new admission after the minimum of 2, the teacher receives Rs. 2,000 per new admission.
  • Example — C-1: New admissions 10, students left 1 → Net new 9 → Eligible: 9−2 = 7 → Total: Rs. 14,000

All Other Teachers (Referrals) — Class 3 & above

  • Condition: Must demonstrate a link between the teacher and parent.
  • For each new admission referred, the teacher receives Rs. 2,000 per admission.
  • Example: 10 referred, 8 with verified link → Total: Rs. 16,000

3. Teacher / Employee Referral Program

  • An existing teacher can refer a fellow teacher for a vacancy.
  • If the referred teacher is deemed fit, hired, and passes the 3-month evaluation period — the referring teacher receives a bonus of Rs. 3,000.
