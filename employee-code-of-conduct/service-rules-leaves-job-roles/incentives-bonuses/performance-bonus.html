Performance Bonus

LMCS distributes a bi-annual bonus based on daily reporting scores provided by Principals, combined with other school assessments.

Bi-Annual Bonus Structure

  • The overall bonus sum and category ratios are decided by the Governing Body.
  • The overall sum is divided based on employee category: Teachers, Admin Staff, and Helping Staff.

Payment Schedule

Evaluated Months Bonus Payable With
May, June, July, August, September Salaries of November, December, February & March (same year)
October, November, December, February, March Salaries of May, June, July & September (next academic year)

Bonus Allocation Split

  • ½ of the overall bonus per category is a fixed bonus — equally divided amongst all employees in that category.
  • The remaining ½ is performance-based, calculated on monthly performance scores.

Performance Bonus Calculation

Follow these steps to calculate the performance bonus for each employee:

  1. Sum all individual performance points across all employees in the category.
  2. Calculate the multiplier: Divide the total performance bonus pool by the sum of individual points, then round down to the nearest 10.
  3. Multiply each employee's individual score by the multiplier to get their performance bonus amount.
