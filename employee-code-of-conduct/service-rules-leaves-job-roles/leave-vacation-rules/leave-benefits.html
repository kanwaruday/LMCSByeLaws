Leave Benefits

LMCS provides two categories of paid leave benefits: Casual Leave (CL) and Compensatory Leave. Both are governed by strict eligibility and usage rules.

Casual Leave (CL)

Rule Details
Entitlement — Teaching Staff 1 Casual Leave per month (12 CL per academic year)
Entitlement — Admin / Helping Staff ½ of every vacation day is counted as CL (see Non-Teaching Staff Vacations)
Nature Paid leave — salary is not deducted when CL is used
Claim Deadline Must be claimed by 31 March of the academic year; unused CL lapses after this date
Carry-Forward Not permitted. CL does not carry forward to the next academic year
On Resignation / Termination Lapses immediately; no encashment of unused CL
Half-Day CL A half-day leave taken against CL is counted as one full CL deducted from the balance

Compensatory Leave

Rule Details
Eligibility Granted when an employee is required to work on a holiday or vacation day at the school's request
Nature Paid leave — equivalent in value to CL
Approval Chain Recommended by the Branch Principal → approved by MD Operations. Compensatory Leave is not valid without MD Operations approval.
Encashment Not permitted. Compensatory Leave cannot be converted to cash
Claim Deadline Must be claimed by 31 March of the same academic year; unused Compensatory Leave lapses after this date
Employees are advised to track their CL balance monthly and plan leave accordingly. The Admin Manager publishes individual leave balance statements at the start of each term.
