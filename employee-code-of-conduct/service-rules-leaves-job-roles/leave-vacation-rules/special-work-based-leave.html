Special & Work-Based Leave

These leave categories are tied to school events, non-compliance, or special circumstances rather than a simple day-off request.

Special Day Leave

A Special Day Leave counts as 2 leave days and is applied when an employee is absent on any of the following high-priority school days:

  • First or last day of an academic term
  • Annual Function day
  • Investiture Ceremony
  • Sports Day
  • External events where school representation is required
  • Combined meetings called by the Principal or Head Office
  • MD Operations meetings / all-staff briefings

Absence on any of the above days without prior written approval from the MD Operations is automatically recorded as a Special Day Leave (2 leave days deducted).

Special Day Absent

If an employee is absent on a Special Day and fails to inform the school by the previous evening's deadline, the deduction escalates to 5 leave days.

Scenario Deduction
Absent on a Special Day (notified in advance) 2 leave days (Special Day Leave)
Absent on a Special Day (no prior notification) 5 leave days (Special Day Absent)

Non-Compliance Leave

Non-Compliance Leave is deducted when an employee fails to complete an assigned responsibility, submit a required document, or follow a school process by its deadline — even if the employee was physically present at school.

Rule Details
Deduction per instance −0.1 leave per non-compliance instance
Conversion 10 non-compliance instances = 1 full leave day deducted
Frequency Calculated and deducted monthly from the employee's leave balance
Examples of non-compliance Missing QP submission deadline, not updating CSM data on time, failing to sign registers, not handing over duties before leave
Non-Compliance deductions are automatically flagged by the System Coordinator and forwarded to the Admin Manager. Employees receive a monthly summary of any deductions recorded against them.
