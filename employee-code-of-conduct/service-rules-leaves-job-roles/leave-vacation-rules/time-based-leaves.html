Time-Based Leaves

Time-based leaves accumulate as fractional leave days based on the duration of partial absence. When sufficient instances accumulate, they convert into full leave deductions.

Late Leave

Rule Details
Definition Arriving after the official reporting time
Grace period Within 5 minutes of the official reporting time — not counted as Late Leave
Conversion rate 4 Late Leaves = 1 full leave day deducted
Recording Recorded in the branch register by the Principal and reported to the Admin Manager weekly

Short Leave

Rule Details
Definition Leaving early or being absent for a part of the working day (less than a half day)
Conversion rate 3 Short Leaves = 1 full leave day deducted
Calculation Proportional to the work-day hours — each hour of absence within the workday is tracked. When 3 such instances are recorded, one full leave is deducted.

Half Leave

Rule Details
Definition Absence covering approximately half the working day (typically the first half or second half of the school day)
Conversion rate 2 Half Leaves = 1 full leave day deducted
Minimum hours requirement The employee must have worked at least the minimum required hours on the day in question for it to qualify as a Half Leave rather than an Absent
