Process of Hiring

Before Interview

  1. An Employee applies either from the Website, Facebook or in person, at the Office.
  2. The entry of such an applicant must be entered into the School Software along with the number on the Application Form.
  3. All of the Applicants must be called for Interview together.

Interview

The following areas must be assessed during the interview:

  1. Spouse's Occupation — Scope of transfer, monetary needs, job stability.
  2. Family Setup — Nuclear vs. joint family for support structure; children's age and school.
  3. Residence — Ease of reaching School.
  4. Academic Qualification — Type of school attended; subject background and combination; Marks in Class 10 & 12; Private vs. Regular degree; Open Schooling; gaps between years; CTET / TET / D.El.Ed / B.Ed.
  5. Professional Skills — Computer literacy: Word, Excel, PowerPoint, General.
  6. Experience — Duration, periods taken, school timing, subjects taught, salary drawn; extra hobbies; dance/music for Annual Function.
  7. Commitment to Serve — Reasons for applying; marital/family status.
  8. Minimum Monthly Salary — Ask for the lowest acceptable salary; evaluate candidates quoting high by cross-checking qualifications.
  9. Overall Attributes — Confidence, English proficiency, strengths & weaknesses; Demo class if required; student feedback.
  10. Subject to performance in the Interview, call for a Demo Class.

Demo Class

  1. Observe the class for:
    • Class Discipline
    • Content
    • Teaching Methodology
    • Creativity in Teaching
  2. Half of the period must be spent observing the teacher through the CCTV cameras for Class Discipline & Changes in Attitude.
  3. Subject to satisfactory performance, the Employee could be hired.

Hiring

  1. The Hiring Form needs to be filled and forwarded to the Head Office along with the Bio-Data Form within 1 week of hiring.
  2. The Salary on the Hiring Form should be filled as per the dashboard given to each Principal.
