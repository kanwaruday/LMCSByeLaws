Fines

ViolationFine
Late submission of Question Paper & Solution Rs. 50 / day / paper
Teacher does not submit QP & Solution till Exam Rs. 1,000 per QP + Rs. 1,000 per Solution = Rs. 2,000 per paper (in addition to the late submission fine)
QP and Solution not in separate files Rs. 50 / paper
Formatting error (font style, size, bold, heading, line spacing) Rs. 50 / paper; Rs. 5 for minor errors
Mistakes in Question Paper Rs. 5 / mistake
Late submission of Answer Sheet Rs. 50 / day / paper
Super-Checking errors Rs. 1 / mistake
Refusal to check allocated Question Papers ½ × (No. of Unchecked Papers ÷ Total Allocated Papers) × Current CTI
Example: 10 unchecked of 100 allocated, CTI = Rs. 15,000 → Fine = Rs. 750
