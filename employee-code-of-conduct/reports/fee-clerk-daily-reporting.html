Daily Reporting by Fee Clerks on WhatsApp

Fee Clerk Daily Reporting

To prevent miscommunication and confusion amongst Fee Clerks, the following reporting protocol is to be followed:

Daily WhatsApp Report

Fee Clerks of LMS 1, 2, 3, 4 and Bhim Sir must send a report of the day by 6:00 PM every evening. The report must include the following sections:

SectionWhat to Report
Hourly Work Done Everything done during each period of time throughout the day
No. of Parents Attended Number of different parents attended during the day
Parent Feedback Summary of major things said by parents — both positive and negative. Fee Clerks are the school's public relations in-charges.
Employee Leave Status Names of employees under each leave category
Outside School Document Received Record of letters, correspondences, etc. received from people/organisations/authorities not part of the school
Cash in Hand
  • Petty Cash — amount of cash with the Fee Clerk for day-to-day school expenses
  • School Fee Cash — if fee has not been deposited that day, the amount of cash from fees
  • Other Cash — cash from other sources (VVM, Scholastic, Annual Fee, etc.)

Substitution by Computer Teacher

  1. If a Fee Clerk is on leave, the Computer Teacher shall be responsible for fees and other minor duties of the Fee Clerk.
  2. Even during substitution, the Fee Clerk shall still fill sections: Employee Leave Status, Outside School Document Received, and Cash in Hand.
