TRR Format (Teacher's Report & Record)

Front to Back — English Style

  1. Monthly Course Map with CWA & other dates (print out from Excel Sheet)
  2. Activity List & Experiential Learning Classes (non-Mylestone classes)
  3. Blueprint — CWAs & Term Exams
  4. June Report (leave 2 pages blank)
  5. February Report (leave 2 pages blank)
  6. Daily Reports

Extra-Curricular Record — Types of Records to Include

  1. Monitor list with dates
  2. All competitions — tick for participation, numbers for positions
  3. Position of responsibilities held (e.g. Sports Captain)
  4. Inter House and Inter School participation & competitions
  5. All Category B competitions in one column
