Regarding Transport Facility (Conveyance)

Students are expected to follow the given norms:

  1. Conveyance once availed cannot be terminated for the current year.
  2. Buses will not wait for late comers.
  3. No student should travel standing on the footboard.
  4. Students must not move around in the bus when it is in motion.
  5. No student should come near the entry door of the bus until it comes to complete halt. The front door of the bus is the only authorised entrance and exit.
  6. Boarding and alighting from buses should be done in silence and in an orderly manner.
  7. Students must make sure that the aisle of the bus is clear, and that school bags and other belongings are placed properly.
  8. Students must keep all parts of their body inside the bus at all times. They should not put their hands out even for waving.
  9. No object should be thrown inside or outside the bus.
  10. The consumption of eatables and drinks is not permitted in the bus.
  11. Unruly behaviour like shrieking and shouting is strictly prohibited; courteous behaviour is expected at all times.
  12. The driver's attention must not be distracted for any reason.
  13. The drivers are authorised to stop buses at designated stops only, unless otherwise directed by the bus teacher.
  14. The bus teacher and the monitor on duty are responsible for maintaining discipline in the bus. Any serious offence will be reported to the Head of School immediately.
  15. Parents/guardians of students who do not avail the School's transport should present themselves at the School gate 10 minutes before the start/dispersal of the School to drop/collect their wards.
