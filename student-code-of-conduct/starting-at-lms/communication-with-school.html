Communication with School

Ways to Communicate

There are several ways of communicating with the School:

  1. Primary Communication with the Teacher is through the Almanac.
  2. Parents can meet the Teacher between 8:45 am and 9:00 am on any School working day.
  3. Parents can visit School for Monthly Open House (MOH) as per dates given on a monthly basis by the Teachers.
  4. Parents can meet the Principal on Monday, Wednesday & Friday between 10:00 am and 11:00 am on School Working Days.
  5. Parents can call the Principal directly on his/her personal mobile number between 6:00 pm and 8:00 pm on all School Working Days.
  6. Parents can WhatsApp, SMS or email the Principal at any time and can expect a response within 24 hours.
  7. School Open House will be held for Half Yearly and Final Exams.
  8. Parents and Students' interest is paramount; in case of any emergency the above norms can be waived off as per the Principal's discretion.

School Email Communications

The School sends frequent emails to students on their official email ID. The following can be expected through Email:

  1. Majority of the information and notifications are sent through email.
  2. Daily Home Work is sent through email.
  3. Question Papers for CWA, OTT, SA (Examinations).
  4. Report Card.
  5. Outsourced vendor assessments.
