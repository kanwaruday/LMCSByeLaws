Use of an Almanac

An Almanac aims at establishing an invaluable link between Teachers, Students and Parents. It is intended to serve multiple purposes:

  • As an all-in-one ready reckoner and planner for students.
  • As a means of communication between Parents and the School.
  • To keep Parents informed about their child's progress at School.
  • To keep Parents informed with respect to assignments for home.
  • To serve as a handy reference with regard to the student's schedule.
  • To be used for convenient, day-to-day correspondence (related to Academics and Conduct) between Parents and the School.

Student's Role

  1. Almanac must be brought to School daily.
  2. Must read all information in the Almanac and abide by it.
  3. Must update the Home Assignment daily.
  4. Performance Report should be updated simultaneously.
  5. Students of Class VI & above must give timely feedback on their teachers.

Parent's Role

  1. Review the current page daily and must countersign.
  2. Check & sign Communication Record on page 4 daily.
  3. Check & sign Performance Reports at the end of the Almanac regularly.
  4. Use Almanac as the primary source of Communication with the Teacher.
