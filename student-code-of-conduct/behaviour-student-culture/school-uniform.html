School Uniform

General Rules

  1. Students must wear their uniforms with pride and ensure that they look neat and presentable at all times.
  2. Winter uniform to be worn from the beginning of the new session and after Dussehra break. Summer uniform from the first Monday of May.
  3. As long as a student is on the School rolls she/he must always come to School in uniform.
  4. Three days a week, students are expected to come in track suits and black sports wear, whereas Montessori Wing can come in casuals.
  5. No student is allowed to attend any Open House, School Function in or outside the School in any dress other than School uniform.
  6. It is mandatory for girls to wear divided skirts with white bloomers (with elastic on the thighs) under the skirts/tunics.
Ref.: HES/MD(Op.)/UK/OO/03/26     Date: 1st January 2026

OFFICE ORDER – Uniform & Grooming Guidelines

A. Colours

Primary colour: Red – RGB (206, 0, 0)

B. Logo Dimensions

  • Sizes up to 32 – Logo Diameter: 5 cm
  • Sizes 34 and above – Logo Diameter: 6 cm

C. Classes 6 to 12 (Formals)

SeasonUniform
Winter White full-sleeve cotton shirt with logo; Dark grey pants (Boys) / Grey divider skirts (Girls); Red tie (3.2–3.5 inches, knotted); Red blazer with golden buttons; Red sweater cardigan (optional); Red socks with 1 cm blue & yellow stripes; LMS School Belt (Red–White–Red with logo buckle); Black sports shoes – Liberty
Summer White full-sleeve cotton shirt with logo; Dark grey pants/divider skirts; Red tie (3.2–3.5 inches); LMS Belt; Red socks; Black shoes

D. Classes 1 to 5 (Formals)

SeasonUniform
Winter White full-sleeve shirt with logo; Dark grey pants/divider skirts; Red tie (2.7 inches, premade); Red sweater cardigan; Red blazer with golden buttons; Red socks with blue & yellow stripes; LMS Belt; Black shoes – Liberty
Summer Boys: Red pants, white shirt with logo, tie, belt, socks, black shoes. Girls: Red tunic with logo over white shirt, tie, belt, socks, black shoes

E. Track Suits (All Classes – Alternate Days)

  • Black track pants with 2 cm red side stripes, "LMS" on pocket
  • Summer: White Polo Shirt with logo (5.5–6.5 cm), black collar + red stripe, sleeve ends black/red bands
  • Winter: Red hoodie (320–350 gsm) with "30 Years of LMS" at the back

F. Montessori Wing (M-1 to M-3)

  • LMS Red Hoodie (320–350 gsm)
  • Winter Hoodie-Puffer Jacket (300 gsm fill, black fleece lining)
  • Black track pants with red stripes
  • Red socks
  • White Polo Shirt (Summer) with LMS Logo
