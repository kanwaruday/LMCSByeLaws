Misconduct of Student

The following escalation process applies for student misbehaviour and misconduct:

  1. On misbehaviour and misconduct in the Class the Teacher must give three warnings.
  2. If the teacher feels that she is unable to control the student, he/she should bring it to the Principal's notice by means of the TPR.
  3. After the third mention, the teacher can request the Principal to counsel the student. He/she must also inform the Parent only through a note in the Almanac about the Principal's Counselling.
  4. After the third counselling by the Principal, the teacher in consultation with the Principal can request for the Parent to be called for the MOH (Monthly Open House).
  5. If the student has not shown any improvement after the parents' third consecutive MOH, the Parent can be called to speak to the Principal.
  6. If the student continues to show signs of indiscipline and resistance to improve, the student can be called for a PTA meeting and addressed by the entire PTA Governing committee.
  7. In case the student still refuses to show any signs of improvement, the LMS Heads Forum can decide to suspend the student for a maximum of the rest of the Academic Term.
In case the Student still refuses to change, the Heads Forum can decide to rusticate the student and the same shall be informed to the PTA Governing Body.
