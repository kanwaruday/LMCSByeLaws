House System

  1. Class I onwards, students are allocated into the following houses:
    💎

    Ruby

    💚

    Emerald

    💙

    Sapphire

    💛

    Topaz

  2. Each House is headed by a House Captain, a House Vice-Captain and a number of House Prefects. The House Moderator is a member of staff who is in charge of each House.
  3. Points are awarded to students of each house throughout the year for their individual and collective performance in academics, co-curricular activities, games and sports.
