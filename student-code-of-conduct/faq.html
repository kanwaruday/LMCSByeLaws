Frequently Asked Questions

Can my child celebrate her/his birthday in School?

Yes, toffees / candies / chocolates costing less than or equal to Rs. 5 are allowed. It is further requested to NOT SEND 'Mithai' / presents, etc. for distribution to the Classmates and the Teachers.

Is my child allowed to carry money to School?

No. Children are not allowed to carry money to School, with the exception of when it is being asked by the Teacher.

What do I need to do to update any personal information?

Parents can either request for any type of change in information using the online portal at www.lms.org.in OR send an application to the respective Class Teacher for any kind of change in information viz. address, telephone / mobile numbers, email ID, Adhaar Card Information etc.

How do I recover my child's lost belongings?

'Prevention is better than cure'. The name, class and section of the student should be clearly marked on all belongings of the students — e.g. blazers, sweaters, ties, belts, water bottles, tiffins etc. In case your ward has lost her/his belongings, please write to your child's class teacher and get the matter resolved.

What if my child forgets to carry her/his lunch box?

Don't worry. If informed in time, the Class Teacher ensures that your child will not go hungry.

What is the procedure if my child has to take leave of absence for a few days?

In case leave is required, the class teacher can be informed through a Leave Application which needs to be duly signed by the authorised parent / guardian. In case of an emergency, the Principal should be informed through phone / SMS / Whatsapp and the leave application must be submitted to the Class Teacher on joining.
