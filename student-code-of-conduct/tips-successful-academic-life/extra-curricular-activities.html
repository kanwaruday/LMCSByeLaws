Role of Extra Curricular Activities

  1. Extra Curricular Activities and sports at LMS are as important as the Education itself.
  2. Extra Curricular Activities, Sports & Competitions ensure a holistically developed child.
  3. The Activities, Sports & Competitions are essential because they help students attain essential 21st Century Skills:
    • Critical Thinking & Problem Solving
    • Creativity & Innovation
    • Collaboration
    • Communication, Information & Media Literacy
    • Cross-Cultural Understanding
    • Computing & ICT Literacy
  4. They make the students confident and self-reliant.
  5. A lot of the activities are designed to help students get acquainted with possible skills, hobbies and interests.
  6. A number of competitions at School are derived from interview and evaluation centres, laying the foundation for competitive exams like UPSC, SSB, IBPS, CAT, SSC, etc.
  7. These also help students explore non-academic career options.

How Competitions are Organised

  1. School Teachers mentor students about various types of extra curricular activities.
  2. A competition is usually held on a Tuesday at the Class level, where teachers give feedback and select the top 3–5 students for the School Level Competition.
  3. Students have a day to improve on the feedback.
  4. A School Level Competition is then held on a Thursday.
