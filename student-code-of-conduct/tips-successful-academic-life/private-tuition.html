Negative Impact of Private Tuition Classes

  1. Tuition Classes in most cases are like slow poison for the School Students.
  2. The tuition classes can destroy a student's Critical Thinking & Problem Solving Powers.
  3. The Students also become dependent on other people for their own work.
  4. Tuition Classes either replace the Self-Study time or the Play time. Reduction in either of these have been known to have a negative impact on the development of the student.
  5. The Tuition Classes also take a toll on a student's family time.
  6. They have also been known to reduce the creativity of a student.
  7. In the senior classes, most students are known to acquire all sorts of bad & negative habits during the travel to the tuition classes.
