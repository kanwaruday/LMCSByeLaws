New Admission Special Cases

1. Inter LMS Migration of Students

  1. A parent can transfer between the Branches of LMS by paying the difference in the New Admission Fee between the Original School Branch and the Destination School Branch.
  2. In case the New Admission Fees of the Original LMS Branch has increased, the latest New Admission fee will be considered to calculate the difference.

2. Returning Students

  1. In the case of a student returning back to LMS from another School, a 50% concession on New Admission Fees can be given.
  2. In the case of a student returning back to LMS from another School within 9 months of leaving the School, a 100% concession on New Admission Fees can be given.

3. Alumni Ward

Acknowledging the significant bond between our institution and our esteemed alumni, we are pleased to extend a 50% discount on New Admission fees for wards of LMS alumni.
