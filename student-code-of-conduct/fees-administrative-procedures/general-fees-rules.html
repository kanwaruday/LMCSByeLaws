General Fees Rules

Fee Rules — La Montessori Chain of Schools

1.1 Payment Deadline & Late Fee

School dues must be paid on or before the 10th of each month. Payment received after the 10th will attract a late fee as follows:

PeriodLate Fee
11th – 15th of the month₹100
16th – last day of the month₹200
Every subsequent month of default₹500 per month

1.2 Fee Counter Timings

DayTimings
School working days9:00 am – 1:30 pm & 2:30 pm – 5:00 pm
School holidays10:00 am – 2:00 pm

1.3 Advance Payment & Annual Discount

Fees may be deposited in advance for any number of months or for the entire academic year. A 5% discount on tuition fees is applicable to parents who pay the entire yearly tuition fee before 30th April.

1.4 Receipt Policy

Important: Fees will be considered paid ONLY upon receipt of an official fee receipt (online or offline). NO RECEIPT = NO FEES PAID.

Parents are requested to use the School's Online Fee Portal: https://onlinefee.lms.org.in/

Payment modes that do not generate an instant receipt — such as RTGS, NEFT, IMPS, UPI, cheque, GPay, Paytm, or PhonePe — are discouraged. If used, an immediate confirmation and fee receipt must be obtained from the Fee Office.

1.5 Authorised Payment Personnel

Dues must be handed over only to the person at the fee counter. Payments made to any other individual will not be recognised.

1.6 Fee Heads & Transport Fee

Tuition fees and conveyance fees are payable for 12 months of the academic year. Transport fee discounts based on days operated:

Days Transport OperatedDiscount
0 – 5 days50% discount
6 – 10 days25% discount
More than 10 daysNo discount

1.7 Inter-Branch Migration

  1. A migration fee is chargeable at the time of transfer.
  2. The fee structure of the destination branch will apply from the date of transfer.
  3. If the New Admission Fee paid at the original branch is lower than that of the destination branch, the parent is liable to pay the difference. No refund is applicable if the amount paid is higher.
  4. Fees already paid for the current academic year at the original branch will be credited towards the destination branch's fee structure. Any deficit is payable; any surplus is non-refundable.
  5. A transfer is considered incomplete until all fees as per the destination branch's fee structure have been cleared.

1.8 Default & Consequences

Default PeriodConsequence
More than 3 monthsStudent will be debarred from appearing in examinations
More than 6 monthsStudent's name will be struck off from the school roll
