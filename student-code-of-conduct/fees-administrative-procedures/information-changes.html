Process for Information Changes

For any changes in personal information including name changes, address, telephone/mobile numbers, email ID, Aadhaar Card Information, etc.:

  1. Parents are expected to request any sort of change by sending an application to the Fee Clerks at the School.
  2. Parents can access student information on the School Website under the Student Login at www.lms.org.in.
  3. Usually it could take up to 1 week for the information to be updated in the School records.
