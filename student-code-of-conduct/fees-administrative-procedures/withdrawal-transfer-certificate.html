For Withdrawal & Transfer Certificate

  1. One month's advance written application or one month's dues in lieu thereof is required when the child is to be withdrawn.
  2. Fees is due so long as no written notice of withdrawal is submitted. Those who leave school in December must in all cases pay the fees for January and February of the coming year.
  3. Parents are requested to collect the security personally, within one month of the issuance of the School Leaving Certificate (SLC), failing which the deposit will be forfeited.
