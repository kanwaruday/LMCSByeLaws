Parent Discounts & Incentives 2026–27

Ref.: HES/MD(Op.)/UK/OO/02/25     Date: 1st January 2026

OFFICE ORDER

For the academic year 2026–27 the following provisions for Discounts and Incentives are approved and applicable.

1. New Parent Referral

  • If "Parent A" refers "Parent B," both will receive a 20% discount on the new admission fees.
  • If "Parent A" refers two or more new admissions, Parent A will get a 40% discount, and each referred parent will get a 20% discount.

2. Old Parent Referral Scheme

An old parent is defined as a parent whose student is either active at the School or is an Alumni of the School.

  • If an old parent refers a new admission, the referred new parent can get a 20% discount on the admission fees. The referring old parent will receive an incentive equal to 10% of the collected new admission fees as a discount on their tuition fees.
  • If the referring old parent chooses not to offer the 20% discount to the referred new parent, they will receive 10% of the total admission fees paid by the referred new parent as a discount on their tuition fees.
