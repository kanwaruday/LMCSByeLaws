Vision & Mission

Vision

"Groom each Child's Potential to Excellence"

Mission

The School's mission is to holistically develop individuals using Child Centric & Life-Skill based education. Also to prepare Students for Challenges of the future.

Essence of the La Montessori Way of Learning

Montessorians believe in allowing the child to develop according to their inner laws of development. There are four distinct planes of human growth:

1st Plane – Montessori Wing (MW)

Child has a very absorbent mind. LMS works on constructivism and independence using tools of a Joyful way of Learning.

2nd Plane – Primary Wing (PW)

Child is in the process of intellectual development. LMS empowers students with foundation laying and inculcates in them a Moral & Social sense.

3rd Plane – Elementary & Senior Wing (EW & SW)

This is the most eventful time. LMS ensures that the students can utilise their creativity and develop holistically. This moulds a child into the adult self.

4th Plane – After Schooling

Schooling at LMS leads a person to be competent, self disciplined and socially well adjusted.

The Montessori Education System is characterized by an emphasis on independence, freedom within limits and respect for a child's natural psychological development as well as technological advancement in society. We believe in a constructivist model for learning rather than instructivist.
